Weekly Library Hours

Trexler Library's hours are subject to change with the College's COVID-19 alert levels. These Weekly Hours reflect the College's current alert level.




Directions
Muhlenberg College is located at 2400 Chew Street in Allentown, PA.  Trexler Library is located on the south side of Chew Street.
Parking is available on Chew Street. Parking in the lots adjacent to the library at any time requires registering the vehicle with the College and displaying a current campus permit or parking pass. Contact Campus Safety for more information on parking.
