Tennis Teams Swept by Ranked Opponents
 Sunday, April 21, 2013







					 


The Muhlenberg tennis teams had a rough weekend playing at Johns Hopkins on Saturday and New Jersey on Sunday, losing all four matches 9-0.

All four Mule opponents have regional and/or national rankings. For the men, Johns Hopkins is eighth and TCNJ is 30th in Division III. In the women’s 
rankings, Hopkins is sixth in the country and TCNJ is 14th in the Northeast Region.

Both Muhlenberg teams are alive for Centennial Conference playoff berths and wrap up their regular seasons this week. The men have CC matches against Dickinson, Franklin & Marshall and Ursinus,
while the women have a non-conference match at Wilkes before hosting Dickinson in a key match on Saturday.
