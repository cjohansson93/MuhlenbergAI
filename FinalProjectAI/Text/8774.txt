SAFETY  PHILOSOPHY
Muhlenberg College is dedicated to the reduction of occupational injury and disease.  As a member of the University and College Insurance Consortium (UCIC) accident and illness prevention program, Muhlenberg College shall take every reasonable action to promote continuous safety awareness as an appropriate mode of behavior to minimize accidents and occupational injuries at all times. The safety program policy serves as the foundation for all program activities.
