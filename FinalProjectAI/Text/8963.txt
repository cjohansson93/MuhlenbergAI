Academic Internship Opportunities and Information
The Career Center is excited to be offering Summer 2021 internship stipends through the Alumni Board Career Scholarship program. You do not need to have an internship secured in order to apply! Here is the application which includes the requirements, and it's due Friday, March 19, 2021. Please feel free to reach out to Pat Fligge, Interim Co-Director of Career Services (pfligge@muhlenberg.edu) with any questions. Your application requires a letter of recommendation, so start thinking about it now!
