"Increasing Contextual Learning in the Environmental Science Classroom" Field Study Modules
For each field study topic in this K-12 program, there is a teacher module/unit plan which includes pre and post trip lessons, background information, copy masters for handouts, supplemental resources, and a list of all PA academic standards covered by the module. The modules are available free for downloading. Several modules also have free downloadable PowerPoint slides that go along with them, which can be used for classroom lessons. In order to view these materials, you will need the following software on your computer:
Click here to download a free copy of Adobe Acrobat Reader.
