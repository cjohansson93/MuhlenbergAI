Welcome to the Business Office

Business Office Staff work to support the mission of Muhlenberg College.
Student services provided by the Business Office are Student Account Billing and Collection, College Cashier, Perkins Loan Reporting and Payroll Services.
College services provided by the Business Office are Payroll Services, Accounts Payable, Cashier Services and Endowment Accounting.
Location: Ground Floor of the Haas College CenterHours: 8:30 am to 5:00 pm, Monday thru FridayPhone Number: 484-664-3150

Effective January 1, 2022 our mileage reimbursement rate will increase from 56.0 cents to 58.5 cents per mile. For travel prior to January 1, 2022 you will be reimbursed at the rate of 56.0 cents per mile. Travel after January 1, 2022 will be reimbursed at the rate of 58.5 cents per mile. You are encouraged to submit your reimbursements in a timely manner preferably within two weeks of return from travel assignments.
This increase in our mileage rate is the rate authorized by the Internal Revenue Service effective January 1, 2022.

Please contact the Business Office at ext. 3150 if you have questions.  Thank you!
