The Path to Healthcare Leadership Starts Here
Lehigh Valley Health Network has partnered with Muhlenberg College School of Graduate Studies to help you achieve the next step in your journey to healthcare leadership. Our versatile Master's in Organizational Leadership (MOL) provides the essentials to become a physician leader who thinks holistically and solves complex problems with empathy, ethics and creativity.
Earn Your Master's in Organizational Leadership in One Year 
Physicians who have completed the LVHN SELECT program (Scholarly Excellence. Leadership Experiences. Collaborative Training.) will receive up to 24 credits for prior learning when enrolling in the Muhlenberg Master's in Organizational Leadership. Through this partnership SELECT graduates can earn their MOL degree in as little as one year.
For more information, please download the full Select Flyer . 


LVHN Scholarship Programs


Undergraduate Scholarship
LVHN employees seeking a Muhlenberg undergraduate degree will receive a scholarship for three tuition-free undergraduate courses 

LVHN'SELECT' program*
Students completing the LVHN' SELECT 'program will receive credit for 6 graduate courses as prior learning credit when enrolling in our Masters of Organizational Leadership. 

Graduate Students**
LVHN employees pursuing a Muhlenberg graduate degree will receive a scholarship of two tuition-free graduate courses.



*Graduate and SELECT scholarships cannot be combined. **Graduate scholarship applies to Master's degree programs and can not be used for graduate certificates.
