Join the CIRCLE OF 1848
Including Muhlenberg in your estate plan entitles you to membership in the Circle of 1848, the honorary society for alumni, parents and friends who made future plans for the College through a trust, an estate, life income plans or other future gifts. 
Named for the year in which Muhlenberg College was established, the society recognizes the vital role benefactors played in Muhlenbergâs emergence as a respected, four-year, residential liberal arts institution and the role they play today in ensuring its continued success. 
Member benefits
Members receive invitations to special events such as the Advent Candlelight Carols and a summer theatre production, a lapel pin, the Circle of 1848 monthly newsletter and listings in various College publications.
To join, contact the Office of Gift Planning at gift.planning@muhlenberg.edu or (484) 523-0349 or complete this membership form to join us immediately. The specifics of your arrangements for the College remain confidential.
