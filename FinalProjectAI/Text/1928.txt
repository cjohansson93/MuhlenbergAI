The graduate certificate in Organizational Leadership provides the necessary skills for professionals to grow as leaders in their organizations, managing change, developing staff, and acting ethically to achieve organizational goals. The certificate can be completed within one year, with a second year option to complete the masterâs degree.
Career Opportunities
Graduates who obtain a Certificate in Organizational Leadership from Muhlenberg College will be able to engage in an increased range of opportunities in their current work setting. Additionally, graduates may consider, among others, careers with the following employers:

Deloitte LLP
Oracle Corporation
Anthem, Inc
Merck & Co., Inc
Johnson & Johnson
Comcast Corporation
Verizon Communications Inc.
Vanguard Group Inc
JPMorgan and Chase & Co
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company

The skills and dispositions you will develop:

Communication and teaching skills
Effective interpersonal presence in organizational and societal settings
Ethical decision-making skills
Integrative learning and interdisciplinary thinking
Personal reflection, vision, ownership of purpose, and life plan
Project leadership and management towards achieving goals

The knowledge you will acquire about organizational work:

Operations and logistics
Organizational networking and partnership
Sustainable competitive performance
Talent and career development and workforce planning
Value propositions for customers and other stakeholders
