Research Assistantship Program
The Research Assistantship Program allows faculty members to hire talented Muhlenberg students on a part time basis (up to 20 hours a week during the summer and up to 12 hrs a week during the academic year) to assist them with the professional work required by their academic discipline. The program provides hands-on experience for Muhlenberg students who want to complement their classroom education with practical work experience and lets them to see some aspect of professional scholarly activity from the inside.
New Applications for this program will be considered after July 1, 2020.
