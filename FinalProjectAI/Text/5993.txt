Lucarelli Leads Golfers at Moravian
 Thursday, September 15, 2011







					 


The Muhlenberg men’s golf team began its 2011-12 year by finishing eighth out of 17 teams at the Moravian Fall Invitational.

Sophomore Christopher Lucarelli, making his debut for the Mules, tied for third with a 5-over-par 76.

Sophomore Chris DiMaio tied for 10th with a 78.
