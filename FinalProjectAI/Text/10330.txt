Muhlenberg College Presents Contemporary Music Festival



The performances in the 2022 festival are organized and sponsored by the Muhlenberg College Department of Music.


	 Tuesday, February 15, 2022  11:44 AM

Image licensed from iStock.
The 2022 Contemporary Music Festival events are supported by the Charles A. and Lorna K. Gruber Lectureship in the Arts. These events are free and open to the public. Visit the College's COVID-19 website for information on public events and the College's current masking policy.
 
Friday, February 25, 7:30 p.m.Sparks, Engines and Waterfalls: Stories from the PianoHolly RoadfeldtEmpie Theatre, Baker Center for the Arts
 
 

 
Tuesday, March 22, 7:30 p.m.[impulse]: New Music for Electric Guitar, Percussion and TechnologyMatt Sargent, Rob Cosgrove and Taylor LongRecital Hall, Baker Center for the Arts
 

Friday, April 1, 7:30 p.m.Out of the Depths: New Music by Andrew Ardizzoia, Assistant Professor of MusicEgner Memorial Chapel
