Seegers Union Digital Signs
Seegers Union has three digital signs that can be used for advertising by Departments and Recognized Clubs and Organizations.  The signs are located in the Main Corridor by the Office of Seegers Union & Campus Events, by the entrance to the Wood Dining Commons, and right outside the Bookstore.  To submit a digital sign for this program, please read the Policy below and then completely fill out the Submission Form.
