Pre K-4 Program
In accordance with the Pennsylvania Department of Educationâs changes to the certification programs, the Education Department offers the Pre K-4 Program beginning Fall Semester of 2010. This program is only open to the Class of 2014 and beyond.
The Pre K-4 certification program is built upon a foundation of field-based experiences and a full complement of special methods courses. The program contains a core of requirements in the area of general studies and a complement of courses in pedagogy and can be completed in conjunction with any major.
Required Cognate Courses

One (1) composition/writing course (satisfied through the First -Year Seminar)
MTH 114: Fundamentals of Mathematics or MTH 116: Shape & Symmetry
One (1) additional mathematics course (MTH 114 or 116 recommended)
One (1) British or American Literature course
One (1) biological science course
One (1) physical science course
GEO 101: World Geography

 
Professional Core Courses

EDU 101: History and Politics of American Education
EDU 104: Educational Psychology: Child Learning and Development
EDU 191: Arts in Education
EDU 201: Introduction to Special Education
EDU 202: Introduction to Early Childhood Education
EDU 204: Integrating Curriculum & Instruction for Young Learners
EDU 212: Inclusive Education for Emergent Bilingual Students
EDU 326: Language and Early Literacy*
EDU 327: Literacy and the Integrated Social Studies Curriculum*
EDU 334: Mathematics Education for Young Learners*
EDU 344: Science Education for Young Learners* *Open to students who have been formally accepted into the program. Please see Admission Requirements for the criteria for Formal Acceptance.

 
Professional Semester

EDU 420: Studies in Professional Education
