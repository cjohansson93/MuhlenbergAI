Volleyball Falls in Three at DeSales
 Tuesday, September 6, 2011 - page 2







					 




The Muhlenberg volleyball team was swept in three sets at DeSales in its final match before hosting the Buttermaker Tournament this weekend.

After dropping the first set, 25-11, the Mules (1-4) opened up an 8-5 lead in the second. Senior Davi Gordon had back-to-back kills at the start of the second set, 
one left-handed (left) and one right-handed.

DeSales came back with an 8-0 run to sieze control of the set and won the third, 25-10.

Senior Brooke McQueen (right) led Muhlenberg with 7 kills, and sophomore Erica Wenzel had a match-high 14 digs.
