 Berg Bulletin/Gateway Message Board Options
 
Post Items
Members of the Campus community may post a message   to be shared via the 'Berg Bulletin emails, and displayed on the Gateway Message Board pages: Student Hub, Faculty & Staff .  
 
Make a Gateway Message Board page your "Start" page
To easily access the Gateway page of your choice (Student Hub, Faculty & Staff) simply choose the link on either page stating, "Make this my Message Board start page".  Going forward you will automatically be directed to your page of choice by typing http://start in your browser, or setting http://start as your default home page.
 
Subscribe to the Berg Bulletin
Students may subscribe to the Berg Bulletin email group by sending an email from their Muhlenberg account to TheBergBulletinStudents+subscribe@muhlenberg.edu.
Staff and Faculty members may subscribe to the Berg Bulletin email group by sending an email from their Muhlenberg account to TheBergBulletinStaffFaculty+subscribe@muhlenberg.edu.
 
Unsubscribe from the Berg Bulletin
Students may unsubscribe from the Berg Bulletin email group by sending an email using their Muhlenberg account to TheBergBulletinStudents+unsubscribe@muhlenberg.edu.
Staff and Faculty members may unsubscribe from the Berg Bulletin email group by sending an email using their Muhlenberg account to TheBergBulletinStaffFaculty+unsubscribe@muhlenberg.edu.
 
Contact
Contact the Support Desk at supportdesk@muhlenberg.edu for assistance.
