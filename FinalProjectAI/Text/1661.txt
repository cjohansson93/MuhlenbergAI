âBerg Recognizes Hunger And Homelessness Awareness Day 



Muhlenberg students will work to educate their peers and the community at large on Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Day, this Wednesday, November 16. 


	 Monday, November 14, 2005  01:59 PM

An information table will be set up in Seegers Union all week.
On November 16 at 6:30 p.m., there will be a Face of Homelessness Panel discussion, where current and former homeless people speak about their experiences with homelessness.
Later that night, students will set up a Shantytown on the College’s front lawn and will sleep out from 9:30 p.m. until the next morning.
