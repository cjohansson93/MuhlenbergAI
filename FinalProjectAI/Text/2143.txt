Alexandra Loeser '16
Alexandra Loeser ’16 is an English major and creative writing minor. Alexandra hopes to work in the publishing industry as an editor of books after graduation.
For the 2015-2016 academic year, Alexandra serves as the presidential assistant to the office of public relations at Muhlenberg College.
