Student illnesses and emergencies at times necessitate additional care being provided at one of the local hospitals. In the event that an emergency admission occurs, the Muhlenberg College staff will make every effort to assist parents in securing hotel reservations during the emergent period of the hospitalization. Parents may contact the following offices for assistance:
Dean of Students Office: 484.664.3182
