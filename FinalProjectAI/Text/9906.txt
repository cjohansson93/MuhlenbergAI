Education: BA, Brandeis University; MA and Ed.D., Teachers College, Columbia University
Dr. Golden teaches Music Theory and Fundamentals of Music and has taught World Music in the School of Continuing Studies at Muhlenberg College. She is an accomplished pianist and accompanies both students and faculty as well as performs on harpsichord with the chamber ensemble. She conducted the MST production of Sound of Music and conducts musicals at Moravian College and DeSales as well. Dr. Golden was Director of Music Education at Moravian College, taught in the Masters of Music Education program at University of the Arts in Philadelphia and at Temple University.
