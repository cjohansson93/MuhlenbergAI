Cano Leads Track and Field Teams at ECACs
 Friday, May 16, 2014







					 




Sophomore Daniel Cano set a personal best in the discus to lead a small contingent for the Muhlenberg track and field teams at the ECAC Championships,
a two-day meet hosted by Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

Cano (right) finished second in his flight and 10th overall with a throw of 147-6. THe previous best for the Centennial Conference discus champion
was 145-7 at The Coach P Open a month ago. 

Senior Nicki Cronin placed 11th in the 1500 meters.

The Mules will find out on Sunday who has qualified for the NCAA Championships, scheduled to begin next Thursday at Ohio Wesleyan.
