Homan Goes 4-2 at Petrofes
 Friday, December 2, 2011







					 



Senior Kevin Homan of the Muhlenberg wrestling team continued to rack up the wins, going 4-2 at the Messiah Petrofes Invitational.

Seeded sixth at 149, Homan was upset by a McDaniel wrestler on a one-point decision in the second round, but he came back to win three straight bouts in the consolation
bracket. 

Homan, who entered the week tied for the Centennial Conference lead in wins, improved his season record to 18-5.
