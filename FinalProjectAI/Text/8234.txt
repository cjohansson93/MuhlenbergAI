Gordon Wrestles in Europe


Muhlenberg wrestler Jake Gordon, a rising sophomore competing in the 174-pound weight class, recently returned from the Division III European Wrestling Exchange trip. 


	 Monday, July 6, 2015  03:00 PM

The USA team that Gordon participated on is organized annually by Bulgarian and American coaches to give DIII wrestlers the opportunity to compete internationally. This year the team was stacked with top DIII talent, including four 2015 All-Americans and several regional placewinners. Read Gordon's account of his experience on muhlenbergsports.com.
