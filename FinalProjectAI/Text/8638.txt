Welcome to the 2021 Fall Semester! 
Muhlenberg College Division of Continuing Education provides quality education for adults, offering a supportive learning environment like no other in the area. We take the time to get to know our students, discuss their goals and help them find the best path to success. 
The fall semester offerings are as follows:

School of Continuing Studies - calendars and schedules
School of Graduate Studies - calendars and schedules
