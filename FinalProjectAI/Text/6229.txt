Mayden Finishes 18th at NCAA Meet
 Saturday, May 26, 2012







					 

 



Junior Samantha Mayden of the Muhlenberg women’s track and field team finished 18th in the 5,000 meters at the NCAA Championships.

Seeded 22nd out of the 22 runners in the field, Mayden crossed the finish line ahead of four other competitors, including one from host Claremont.

Above and below are screen caps from the NCAA streaming video of the race. Mayden is in the all-red uniform.
