Possible Pathways for the 4+1 Program
The following lists of courses in each pathway include only the needed undergraduate courses (and the course prerequisites) students can use to count toward the 4+1 program. Additionally, students earning a B or better in the following undergraduate courses would demonstrate the level of proficiency needed to satisfy the requirements for the following graduate courses:

(Earning a B or better)

 The following pathways would include any Muhlenberg Student whose major & minor are outside the Mathematics and Computer Science Department. 
 Any Major + Combination of Undergrad & Grad Courses

MTH 119 could count as RG course
 MTH 219 could count as SC course
MTH 319 general elective for BS degree
MAA 508 general electives for BS degree
MAA 512 general electives for BS degree

 Any Major + All undergraduate courses

 MTH 119 or CSI 10X can count as RG
 MTH or CSI course can count as SC
 All 5 additional courses would be electives (4 if they tested out of CSI 10X)

 Any Major + All Graduate Courses

MAA 502 +MAA 506 + MAA 508 + MAA 512 would count as 4 electives in BS degree
