Audio-Visual Classroom (B-02) Policy





Usage
This room is restricted to class use only. Reservations required.
Reservations
Faculty may reserve this room by calling the Library's A/V Assistant (ext. 3501). Date, time and film to be viewed are required at time of booking. If instructor is unable to attend scheduled viewing, please notify the A/V Assistant; arrangements will be made for library staff to begin film. Room is reserved on a first come, first-served basis.
