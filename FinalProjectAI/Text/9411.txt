An Open Door for Transfer Success
Welcome!
The Transfer Team at Muhlenberg is here to help you through the transfer application process. We value your previous college experience and invite you to learn about all the exciting possibilities Muhlenberg has to offer. 
Each semester we enroll full-time transfer students who have demonstrated academic achievement in high school and college, quality of character and the potential to contribute to our community in meaningful ways.
We have gathered some information that will be helpful to you during this process.
