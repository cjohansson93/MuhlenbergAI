Michael Stehlin, Lectureremail: stehlin@muhlenberg.eduB.A., Providence CollegeM.B.A., Pepperdine University




Michael Stehlin holds a BA from Providence College and an MBA from Pepperdine University.
He joined the Business Program faculty in 2013 after ten years as an adjunct faculty member at Muhlenberg, DeSales University and Cedar Crest College. Prior to coming to Muhlenberg, Stehlin spent over 25 years in industry, holding a variety of Sales, Marketing and Marketing Management positions in the chemical and scientific markets.
Stehlin primarily teaches Marketing, Management and Family Finance and has developed Special Topic courses in Business Marketing and Services Marketing. He also teaches Marketing Management in the Accelerated program of Muhlenbergâs Wescoe School, and advisor to the Business ePortfolio program.
