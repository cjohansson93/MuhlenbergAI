The Final Exam Schedule for Spring 2022 is now available. Faculty are reminded that they are not allowed to change either the date or time of a final examination. These requests must go through the Provost.
ALL STUDENTS, FACULTY, AND STAFF SHOULD PLAN TO BE AVAILABLE UNTIL THE SEMESTER CONCLUDES AT 3:00 P.M. ON FRIDAY, MAY 13TH.
Students - your schedule can be found by logging into Workday. Click on View My Courses. On the next page you will see your enrolled courses. Click on one of your courses within the column labeled Section (NOT the column labeled Course Listing). At the bottom of that page you will see the Date, Time and Location for the exam associated with that course. You will need to click on each course to see the exam details for your course.

Confirm with your instructor specific plans for culminating experiences before reporting any conflict
If there is no final listed for a specific course, you may confirm with your instructor.

Faculty - you can find your exam times in Workday by utilizing the Course Offerings report and clicking on the course. You will find the Date, Time and Location at the bottom of the page under Assessments. You may also access the details via the âView My Teaching Scheduleâ task on your Teaching Dashboard. If you click on the link in the Assessments column for each course, the Date, Time and Location are displayed.
