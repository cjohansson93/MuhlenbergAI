Course Transfer Information
To recieve credit toward the Philosophy major or minor for a transfer course, course abroad, or other non-Muhlenberg philosophy course, the following materials should be submited to the department chair:

Course Description – including course title, number, college/university, duration, general content.
Reading List – to give some idea of texts, authors, types of readings.
Assignments – to give some idea of types of assignments, methods of assessment, etc.
