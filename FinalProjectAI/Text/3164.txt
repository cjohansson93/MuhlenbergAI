kein ranked ninth


Senior Rob Kein of the Muhlenberg wrestling team in ranked ninth in Division III at 149 pounds by the National Wrestling Coaches Association.

Kein is 8-2 on the season, winning his bracket at the Monarch Invitational and finishing second at the Ursinus Fall Brawl. His only losses are in overtime to the
sixth-ranked wrestler and a medical forfeit in the first round of the Messiah Petrofes Invitational. One of his wins came against a 2008 national qualifier.

For his career, Kein is 74-21 with 11 major decisions, seven technical fall and seven pins. This is the second straight year he has appeared in the national rankings;
last season he was ranked as high as eighth.
