Elaine Martin 
Flute
Background B.M.E., Temple University
Musical Director of the Muhlenberg Flute Ensemble.  Flute study with Carole Morgan, Kenton Terry, and Deborah Carter.  Extensive Alexander Technique study with Stanley Tucker.  Former Principal Flutist of the Allentown Symphony Orchestra.  Member of Elan, flute and harp duo.  Cedar Crest College, instructor of flute.  Community Music School of Allentown, instructor of flute and director of Silver Winds Flute Ensemble.
