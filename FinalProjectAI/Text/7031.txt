Men's Tennis Competes At Regionals
 Sunday, September 29, 2013







					 




All three doubles teams won at least one match when the Muhlenberg men’s tennis team competed at the ITA Southeast Regional at Mary Washington this weekend.

The teams of senior Ben Berkowitz and sophomore Derrick Eng, at B1 doubles, and freshman David Woods and junior
Keith Beizer, at B2 doubles, both won their first-round matches before falling in the second round. In the championship draw, sophomore
Max Blumenthal and senior Jason Teitelbaum won a consolation match but had to retire in the next round.

Berkowitz (left) and Woods both won first-round singles matches as well, while Eng captured one consolation match. The Mule to advance the farthest
in his draw was sophomore Neil Shulman, who won his first two matches in B7 singles before dropping a three-set match in the semifinals.
