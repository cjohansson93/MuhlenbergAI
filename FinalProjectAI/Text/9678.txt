Seminar Presentation PPT template

PPT template for seminar presentations by Dr. Keri Colabroy. Students may use this template to assist in their preparation of seminar presentations of their research or scholarly work. 
 









PPT Templates for Poster Presentations
Download a poster template. Instructions for submission of the completed poster are included in the pptx file. 


Natural Science or Social Science Poster Template - a poster template for a Science or Social Science project. 


Humanities Poster Template - a poster template for a Humanities project. 


These templates are *.pptx files created by Dr. Keri Colabroy. You must save the file as a PDF and as a PPTX/PPT and upload both versions to the assignment in Canvas. 
For the 2021 poster session your registration  is due by October 1, 2021
Your poster is due in Canvas by October 23, 2021
Dr. Colabroy will arrange for your posters to be printed. Students are not responsible for printing costs.
