Muhlenberg Career Speakers
Are you an expert in your field? Share your knowledge with fellow Mules and be a career speaker. Your area of expertise might relate to your career field, your educational background, or life experiences. Speakers might be used in our webinar series, during alumni week or as a part of other career events. Contact Chiquita Griffin, Dreictor of Employer and Alumni Engagement chiquitagriffin@muhlenberg.edu for more information.
