Ronny Quevedo Presents 'Space of Play, Play of Space' at Martin Art Gallery



The exhibit, which runs through November 2, 2019, is free and open to the public.


By:

 

  Jonathan Schlegel  


	 Monday, September 23, 2019  10:05 AM


Quevedo will give an artist talk on October 15 at 7 p.m. in the Recital Hall of the Baker Center for the Arts, adjacent to the Martin Art Gallery. The talk is a featured event of Borders, Nationalisms, Identities, the 2019 Center for Ethics program theme.
The Martin Art Gallery is open Tuesday through Saturday 12-8 p.m., and The Martin Art Gallery's Galleria Space is open Monday through Sunday, 9 a.m.-11 p.m. All exhibitions and programming are free and open to the public. For further information, please call 484-664-3467 or visit the Martin Art Gallery website.
