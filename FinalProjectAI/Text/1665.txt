Information Session At The Wescoe School Of Muhlenberg College 



The Wescoe School of Muhlenberg College will host an information session for prospective adult students on Wednesday, January 11 from 6:00 to 7:30 p.m. in the Hoffman House at 23 rd and Chew Sts. on the Allentown campus.


	 Monday, November 28, 2005  01:59 PM

The event opens with a general overview of all programs/majors from 6:00 to 6:30 p.m. From 6:30 to 7:30, visitors will have the opportunity to meet with academic advisors and a representative from the financial aid office. Information about continuing education in Liberal Arts and Accelerated Degree programs, Teacher Certification, Associate’s Degrees, Post-Baccalaureate Certificates and more will be available. In addition, attendees may elect to fill out a complimentary career/skills assessment and enjoy refreshments and informal conversation with Wescoe school staff and instructors.
For more information or driving directions, please call 484-664-3300 or visit www.muhlenberg.edu/wescoe.
