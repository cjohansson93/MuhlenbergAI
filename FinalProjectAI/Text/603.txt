The OIT Help Desk provides technical support to the entire campus community. We are staffed with Client Support Specialists and Student Help Desk Associates who are available to answer your questions and resolve your technical issues.

OIT Help DeskEttinger Hall, Room 002 484.664.3566 helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu


Office Hours: Monday  - Friday, 8:00am - 5:00pmWeekend and evening hours vary (based on student staff scheduling)

Visit the IT Support Portal (available in the OneLogin Application Portal) to report an issue, submit a service request, check on My Items or search the knowledge base.


Call us at 484-664-3566 to speak with a Client Support Specialist or Student Help Desk Associate. Note: After hours  - leave a detailed message and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
Email us - helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu


Need in person assistance? Contact us by email or phone to schedule an appointment. Drop-ins are not currently allowed. Virtual appointments are preferred, whenever possible. In-person assistance is available by appointment, as needed. 

Perhaps you are unable to login? 
Report an Issue (no login required) - Please include your Name and Contact Info
Student Help Desk Associates
The Help Desk is staffed by student Support Associates. These students are trained to help you with your questions about supported software. They will not be expected to answer questions about non-supported software (any programs not on the Muhlenberg network) or commercial on-line services.
Interested in working at the Student Help Desk as a Help Desk Associate? Apply online via Handshake (app available in the application portal), or email helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu.
