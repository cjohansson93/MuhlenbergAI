Borrowing Policies



Library Home|Borrowing|Borrowing Policies






Trexler Library exists to meet the needs of the students, faculty, and staff of Muhlenberg College.
Trexler Library divides patrons into four categories. Please review each description to see which patron type you are.
Student Patrons:
Student patrons are composed of Muhlenberg students (full-time or part-time) and Muhlenberg senior scholars. Please see the Student Patron Borrowing Policy to view the full borrowing rules for our Student patrons.
Faculty/Staff Patrons:
Faculty/Staff patrons are composed of Muhlenberg Faculty/Staff (current or retired), members of the Board of Trustees, Family/Spouse of Muhlenberg Faculty/Staff, and the Allentown Art Museum employees. Please see the Faculty/Staff Patron Borrowing Policy to view the full borrowing rules for our Faculty/Staff patrons
Affiliated Patrons:
Affiliated patrons are composed of Muhlenberg Alumni, Clergy, Muhlenberg Board of Associates. Muhlenberg Alumni are entitled to reference assistance and Interlibrary Loan. Please see the Affiliated Patron Borrowing Policy to view the full borrowing rules for our Affiliated patrons.
Non-Affiliated Patrons:
Non-Affiliated patrons are composed of LVCIL, PALCI, LVAIC, and Town Patrons. Please see the Non-Affiliated Patron Borrowing Policy to view the full borrowing rules for our Non-Affiliated patrons.
Reviewed 2021
