From the Studio of Patricia Budlong:






From the Studio of Ellie Escher:Katherine Conlon, Class of 2024, performs Elizabeth Lehmann's (1862-1918) "There are Fairies at the Bottom of our Garden." In this Liza Lehmann piece, a little girl divulges into the details about a group of fairies that inhabit the garden behind her home. As the song carries on, she reveals her relation to these mystical creatures in a joyful and vibrant conclusion.
