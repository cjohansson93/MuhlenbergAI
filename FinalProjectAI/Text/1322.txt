To successfully transfer to Muhlenberg College, a list of items to complete, including links to forms and websites have been provided below. Please note the various due dates for each item. Your Orientation Leaders will continue to communicate these priorities to you.

Health Form FAQ



In preparation for your arrival to campus, learn about our Health Form and understand the types of information Muhlenberg requires.




Learn More



















Health Forms



Health forms must be submitted prior to moving onto campus. Without your forms, you cannot have access to your Residence Hall. Visit Muhlenberg's OneLogin and select PyraMed Health Portal to fill out your Health Form.




Submit your Health Forms


















Submit Your Housing Application



We’re interested in learning about your housing preferences. Complete this questionnaire at your earliest convenience so that we can begin preparing for your arrival. Please note that these are preferences, and we will not be able to guarantee that one of your top requests will be assigned. You will be contacted by Housing & Residence Life between July 27-31 to discuss your preferences. Your housing assignment will be finalized by August 10.




Submit Application


















Dining on Campus - Meal Plans



Muhlenberg’s dining program offers variety, convenience, and flexibility to students living on and off-campus. Campus meal plans are designed to fit the lifestyles of Muhlenberg students. Fall 2022 meal plans are not yet available.

Confirm Omnilert Contact Information

Incoming Student Orientation 2021 Canvas Course



Beginning approximately Monday, August 2, you will be provided access to a Canvas course for you to review critical information and complete tasks prior to your arrival, including the Student Code of Conduct, FERPA, AlcoholEDU, Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and others! You can log on to Canvas through OneLogin.
