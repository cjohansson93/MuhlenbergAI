Need Help in Workday? 
 


If you need assistance with: Please contact: 




Students who need help with: Academic Dashboard, academic progress, registering for classes, third-party permissions, updating student profile, etc. 

registrar@muhlenberg.edu
 



Faculty and Academic Staff who need help with: Advisor Dashboard, finding advisee/student information, academic administrative tasks, student holds, overrides, teaching schedules/rosters, etc.
registrar@muhlenberg.edu
 



Human Resources-related issues, such as: benefits, time tracking, PTO, etc.
hr@muhlenberg.edu
 




 Business Office-related issues, such as: expenses, student accounts & billing, etc. 


businessoffice@muhlenberg.edu
 




Purchasing-related issues, such as: purchase orders, purchase requisition, etc.


purchasing@muhlenberg.edu
