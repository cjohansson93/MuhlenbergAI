The Red Devils (5-2-2, 1-1-1) scored midway through the first half, giving the Mules (5-0-3, 1-0-2) their first deficit of the season. 

After getting outshot 9-3 in the first half, Muhlenberg came out firing in the second, tying the score seven minutes into the period when junior Cody Antonini took a deflected pass in the middle of the field, beat a defender and beat the goalie for his 11th goal of the season.

The score remained 1-1, although both teams had good chances. Senior Jason Daniels made a career-high 12 saves, including four in quick succession late in the second half. Daniels added three more saves in the second overtime, and a Red Devil shot snuck just wide of the post with time running down.

Muhlenberg snapped a three-game losing streak in Carlsisle and is now undefeated (10-0-5) in its last 15 regular-season games.
