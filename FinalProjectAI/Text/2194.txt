Alumni Profiles




 

Collette Campbell  Class of 2014 Major in TheatreMinor in Dance
Actor, Singer, Dancer




After graduation, Collette moved out to Oregon for a year to be an apprentice for Portland Playhouse. The apprenticeship culminated in a one-woman clown piece called, Home, Sweet, _____ with original music written by fellow mule alum Sean Skahill '15. Collette has performed Home, Sweet, _____ at venues around New York City while continuing to develop it. Now it will be performed at the 2018 FringeNYC - The New York International Fringe Festival, Oct. 13-22, 2018
Collette continues to work as an actor in NYC, having performed at theatres such as Theater for the New City and The Brick Shakespeare Festival, and most recently with Tut'Zanni Theatre Company, in a site specific commedia dellâarte show. In addition, Collette is the lead singer of Catfished, a classic rock cover band in NYC.
