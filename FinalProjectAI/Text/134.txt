Bill Keller
Senior Director of Communicationbillkeller@muhlenberg.edu484-664-3230
Bill Keller, under the direction of the vice president of communications, helps coordinate strategic communications for the College. He works with communications colleagues to develop, share and promote editorial content for Muhlenberg, and he is the liaison for the College's contracted photographers. His team includes Muhlenberg's senior writer, videographer, assistant director of communication for social media and online writer & content editor.
He is a member of the College's web team, crisis management team, digital learning team and the lectures & forums committee.
He graduated from York College of Pennsylvania with a bachelor of arts in professional writing and is completing a master of arts in professional & digital media writing at East Stroudsburg University.
