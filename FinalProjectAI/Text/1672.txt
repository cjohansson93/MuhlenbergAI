Newman Center (Your Home Away From Home)
During the COVID-19 Pandemic, students are asked to use the Newman Center in accord with current College COVID-19 guidelines. 
The heart of Catholic Campus Ministry is the Newman Center.  The Newman Center is a place where members can relax, build community, pray and share their faith and fellowship.
The Newman Center offers the following amenities:

 A Chapel with the Blessed Sacrament reserved for adoration and prayer
 Cable TV with TiVo
 Snacks, Tea and Coffee
 Berg Wi-Fi
 A full kitchen for cooking your favorite homemade meals
