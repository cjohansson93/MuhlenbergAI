Audrey Simons ‌
Cello
BackgroundB.M. Susquehanna University (magna cum laude); M.M. Temple University (summa cum laude)
Studies with Jeffrey Solow, Heidi Jacob and Nancy Bidlack.  Performances with Allentown Symphony Orchestra (tenured musician), Pennsylvania Strings Ensemble, Aviva Strings, founding member of Classical Attitude String Quartet and Chestnut Hill Chamber Players.  Assistant Music Director of Pocono Youth Orchestra and Pocono Junior Strings Orchestra.
