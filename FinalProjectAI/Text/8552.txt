Get Involved
Our Majors participate in a range of student-run media across campus, and they would love for you to join them.










Muhlenberg Broadcast Channel (MBC)
MBC (Channel 21) is a student run organization dedicated to showcasing student works and to bringing informative and entertaining programming to the Muhlenberg campus and on YouTube.



Learn More














Muhlenberg Film Association (MFA)
MFA, is the home of filmmakers and film-lovers to gather, talk, watch, and collaborate.



Learn More













The Muhlenberg Weekly Newspaper
An independent student-run newspaper of Muhlenberg College. The staff of The Weekly will strive to cover important topics affecting the Muhlenberg College Community.



Learn More













WMUH Radio Station
WMUH provides a space for students of Muhlenberg College as well as community staff members to develop, amplify and extend a variety of voices on campus and beyond.



Learn More

Special Programs
Get Engaged
The Department maintains a number of special programs throughout the year that bring our faculty, staff, majors and alumni together.










Alumni Week



A week-long event each year, where successful Muhlenberg Media & Communication and Film Studies Alumni network and hold workshops with current students.

Honors



The Honors Program allows qualified students the opportunity to enrich their experiences in media and communication theory, history, and practice.

Dublin: Study Abroad



A semester abroad experience designed for Media & Communication and Film Studies students at Dublin City University (DCU), Ireland.
