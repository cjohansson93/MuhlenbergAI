Retiree Benefits
Official Retirement from the College is available upon attainment of age 60 and completion of at least 10 years of full-time service. The benefits outlined in the documents provided below are summaries only and are subject to change.
Retiree ID Card: Just prior to retirement or immediately thereafter, retirees may obtain a Retiree ID badge from Seegers Union. Please call ext. 3494 to arrange for an appointment.
Retiree Benefit Summary
Retiree Brochure
Please contact Human Resources (ext. 3165 or hr@muhlenberg.edu) with any questions specific to your retirement situation. Thank you for your service!
