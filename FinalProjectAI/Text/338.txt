Computer and Software Purchases
HP and Lenovo business laptops are commonly available via a number of retailers as well as via their respective websites. Dell computers are also available at special academic prices. Visit  www.dell.com/en-us/     [Member ID:BS27825200]. Visit the Apple Education store for student discounts. 
Note: Microsoft Office will be available for free for all students via a Muhlenberg self-service portal. You must have access to your college email.  Please go to http://msoffice.muhlenberg.edu to access the portal.
Many other software packages are available at discounted prices to students. Please go to journeyed.com for a full catalog of options. Proof of academic eligibility is required.
Warranty Choose a four year warranty, and include coverage for accidental damage.  Know how and where to get the computer serviced if something fails. It is a good idea to keep a copy of the warranty information and the computer manufacturer's tech support number with the computer.
Got questions? Contact the Student Help Desk.
