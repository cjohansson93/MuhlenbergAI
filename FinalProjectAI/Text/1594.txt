	 Thursday, April 21, 2005  09:54 AM

Francine Roussel, assistant professor of theatre, will be in the movie "The Interpreter" starring Nicole Kidman and Sean Penn.  The movie will be released nationwide April 22. 
Roussel, who is French, Roussel appears as the French interpreter in this political thriller filmed on location at the United Nations in New York City.  She also trained Kidman for an important scene where she is on the phone speaking French.
If you live in the Philadelphia area and are interested in hearing about Roussel’s experience working on this movie, tune in to the 10! Show on NBC 10 on Monday, April 25, at 10 a.m., to see her interviewed live!
For more details on the film, visit the movie homepage.
