Physics Major Requirements
Required Courses:


PHY 121 General Physics I
PHY 122 General Physics II
PHY 213 Modern Physics
PHY 216 Analog & Digital Circuits
PHY 241or PHY 242 Thermal & Statistical Physics
PHY 319 Analytical Mechanics
PHY 329 Electromagnetism
PHY 450 CUE: Advanced Projects
One other course numbered between PHY 200 and PHY 399 in the department.






MTH 121 Calculus I




MTH 122 Calculus II





Two additional courses in mathematics chosen from:





MTH 223 Calculus III




MTH 226 Linear Algebra






MTH 227 Differential Equations


 



Students interested in graduate work in physics or engineering will be encouraged to elect additional physics and mathematics courses.
