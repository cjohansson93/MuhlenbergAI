Getting Started
How do I complete my New Student Onboarding tasks in Workday?




Log into the OneLogin application portal using the credentials you received in the Admissions Application portal and click on the Workday tile.
Visit the Office of Information Technology site for details on logging into the OneLogin portal for the first time.




Complete Onboarding Tasks In Your Workday Inbox
Once you are logged into Workday, you can navigate to your Inbox to begin completing the following New Student Onboarding tasks. For a detailed walkthrough, please view this video tutorial or help guide.

Review My Friends and Family
Manage Permissions for my Third Party
Review My Legal Name
Review My Home Contact Information
Review My Personal Information
Complete Health Forms
Print Generated Document
Financial Aid Link





Review Course Offerings for Fall
As you prepare for your Summer Advising meeting with your advisor, we suggest reviewing the Fall 2022 Semester course offerings available in Workday to begin thinking about what courses you may be interested in taking during your first semester at Muhlenberg.
