Education Department Faculty
Faculty in the Education Department are expert teachers who come from a variety of disciplinary backgrounds. They have extensive experience in schools and stay on the cutting-edge of educational policy and research relevant to their teaching, so students have access to the most up-to-date knowledge in the field of education. As one Alum recently said, 
The professors are wonderful mentors that will push you to reach your full potential. This program will put you head and shoulders above every other job candidate. The faculty craft their courses and learning experiences to give you the skills and insights you need to excel.
