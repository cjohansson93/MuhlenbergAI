Dear Student,
Muhlenberg College Division of Graduate & Continuing Education provides quality education for adults, offering a supportive learning environment like no other in the area. We take the time to get to know our students, discuss their goals and help them find the best path to success. There is no one-size-fits-all at Muhlenberg.
With a wide range of degree credit courses taught by a top-notch faculty, you will feel right at home at Muhlenberg.
Peruse our course schedules or reach out to our advising team and let us know how we can help. 
We look forward to meeting you!
AJ Lemheney, Ed.D., Vice President and Executive DirectorJane E. Hudak, M.Ed. Dean, School of Continuing Studies and Summer StudyDavid Donnelly, Ph.D, Dean, School of Graduate Studies
