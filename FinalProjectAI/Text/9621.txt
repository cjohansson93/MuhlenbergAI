Volleyball Sweeps for Fifth Straight



The Muhlenberg volleyball team overcame a slow start with a big rally and went on to sweep FDU-Florham, 3-0, for its fifth straight win to start the season.


	 Thursday, September 7, 2017  01:14 PM


Reigning Centennial Conference player of the week Kelly Tornetta led the Mule attack with 12 kills and hit .370. Sophomores Maggie Enestvedt and Tara Register added 7 kills apiece, and junior Shannon Hubert finished with 32 assists, 12 digs and 5 kills in 7 attempts.
Register (pictured above) tied her career high with 14 digs, and freshman Maya Illovsky recorded 12 digs.
This article was originally published on MuhlenbergSports.com, your home for Mules Athletic news.
