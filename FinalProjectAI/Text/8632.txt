Muhlenberg College School of Continuing Studies2400 Chew StreetAllentown, PA 18104-5586

484-664-3300 / continuingstudies@muhlenberg.edu
The School of Continuing Studies offices are located in the Gabriel House in the middle of the campus green, on Chew Street across from the Trexler Library.

From I-78 (and Route 309, which merges with I-78 near Allentown): Coming east or west, take the Cedar Crest Boulevard Exit (Exit 55; also marked as Route 29). Follow Cedar Crest Boulevard north (towards Allentown) to the sixth traffic light. Turn right onto Chew Street and follow Chew Street one mile to the campus.
From Route 22:  Take the Cedar Crest Boulevard Exit. Follow Cedar Crest Boulevard south to the third traffic light if coming from 22 East, to the fourth traffic light if coming from 22 West. Turn left onto Chew Street and bear right at the fork. Follow Chew Street one mile to campus.
From Northern New Jersey: Take the New Jersey Turnpike or Route 287 to I-78. Follow I-78 west to Pennsylvania and follow directions from I-78 to the campus (above).
From Philadelphia and Southern New Jersey: Entering Pennsylvania, take either the Walt Whitman or Ben Franklin bridge to the Schuylkill Expressway (I-76). Follow the Schuylkill Expressway north to the Northeast Extension I-476. Take the Northeast Extension north to the Lehigh Valley Exit. Take Route 22 east and follow directions from Route 22 to the campus (above).
From Scranton and North: Take the Northeast Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike (I-476, formerly Rt. 9) south to the Lehigh Valley Exit. Take Route 22 east and follow directions from Route 22 to the campus (above).
From Harrisburg and West:                                     Take Route 81 to I-78 east; this is shortly joined by Route 22. When I-78 and Route 22 diverge near Allentown, take Route 22 and follow directions from Route 22 to the campus (above).
