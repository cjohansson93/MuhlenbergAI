Dish Network International TV
International TV programming is currently available in six languages, including French, German, Hebrew, Italian, Russian, and Spanish via TVs in Ettinger suites 101 and 102 as well as all computers in the LC Commons (Ettinger 103).
We are planning to add Arabic and Chinese in phase two of the project. The project is sponsored by the Office of the Provost, OIT, and the Department of Languages, Literatures and Cultures.
For more information and weekly TV Guides, click the image below:                                       
SCOLA International News
SCOLA is a non-profit broadcasting company which supplies authentic foreign language programming to educational organizations for the purposes of supporting foreign language instruction and regional studies, and improving global understanding. SCOLA is currently available to Muhlenberg College student body, faculty, and staff via internet videostream.
To access SCOLA, click the image below:
LiveTV Online
LiveTV is a website with hundreds of TV stations that can be watched online for free. Browse stations by category or by country, or search by station title. A TV toolbar is also available for Firefox, Internet Explorer, and Safari.
