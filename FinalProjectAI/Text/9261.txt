Muhlenberg Integrated Learning Abroad

Unless otherwise stated, all faculty-led programs off campus (MILA, etc.), are subject to the Global Education Policies. This includes the policy of a nonrefundable deposit due upon registration of the program.Provides students with the opportunity to participate in courses that include short-term international study components.  Some MILA courses meet on campus during the fall or spring semester and they include a faculty-led short-term study abroad trip after the close of the semester. Others are faculty-led summer courses abroad.
