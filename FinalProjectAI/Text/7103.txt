Men's Golf 13th at F&M
 Monday, September 23, 2013







					 




The Muhlenberg men’s golf team finished 13th out of 19 teams at the Franklin & Marshall Fall Invitational.

Senior Christopher Lucarelli led the Mules with a 79, four strokes ahead of classmate Chris DiMaio (right).
