The College offers an interdisciplinary physical science major. The physical science major is specifically designed to meet the core courses required for students interested in pursuing the Pre-Engineering Combined Plan programs with Columbia University.For more detail please see the college catalog.















Muhlenberg Advantage
Columbia University Dual Degree Program



Muhlenberg partners with Columbia University to offer a competitive program in engineering and applied science. Graduate with dual bachelor degrees from each institution in as little as five years.
