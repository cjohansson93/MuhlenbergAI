volleyball team honored for academics








The Muhlenberg volleyball team was honored by the American Volleyball Coaches Association (AVCA), earning the AVCA Team Academic Award for the 2007-08 season.

The award recognizes collegiate and high school volleyball teams that have maintained at least a 3.30 cumulative team grade-point average (out of 4.0) during the school year.
 
62 Division III schools were honored, surpassing last year’s record of 54. Muhlenberg was one of only two schools from the Centennial Conference to be honored.

Muhlenberg will receive a plaque and each player on the volleyball team will receive a certificate.
