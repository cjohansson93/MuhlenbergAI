Men's Golf 10th at Mason-Dixon, Men's Tennis Falls in N.Y.
 Sunday, October 6, 2013 - page 2







					 





The Muhlenberg men’s golf team finished 10th out of 15 teams at its first two-day tournament of the year, the Mason-Dixon Classic hosted by McDaniel.

Senior Christopher Lucarelli shot a 79 in both rounds to lead the Mules with a 158. Muhlenberg’s low round of the tournament was 78,
shot by sophomore Jonathan Graulich in the first round and senior Chris DiMaio (right) in the second.

Men’s tennis wrapped up its fall season with a 6-3 loss at Oneonta State yesterday. 

After getting swept in three close doubles matches, the Mules
split in singles. At No. 2, senior Jason Teitelbaum (left) won a marathon match, splitting the first two sets, both of which went to 
tiebreaks, before eking out a 10-8 win in the third set.

Sophomore Neil Shulman (No. 5) and junior Keith Beizer (No. 6) won their singles matches in straight sets.
