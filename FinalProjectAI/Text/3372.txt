Sixteen Muhlenberg College student-athletes were recognized for
their performance in the classroom and on the field with their selection to the
Centennial Conference Academic Honor Roll for the winter 2008-09 season.

To be named to the Academic Honor Roll, a student must be a sophomore or
higher in class standing; a starter or key reserve on his or her team; and
carry at least a 3.40 cumulative grade-point average.

Following is an alphabetical list of the Muhlenberg students who were
honored, with their class year, sport and major(s) and Mule seniors who
made the Academic Honor Roll each of the last three winter seasons
listed in bold:

• Alexis Bates, Junior, Basketball, Spanish/Business Administration
• Kim Bier, Sophomore, Track and Field, Accounting
• Lauren Boyle, Senior, Basketball, Mathematics
• Lucy Brock, Senior, Track and Field, Art/Finance
• Debra Crockett, Junior, Track and Field, Political Science/Business Administration
• Bethany Enterline, Senior, Basketball, Political Science/Business Administration
• Alex Faust, Junior, Track and Field, Dance/Business Administration
• Colin Gibson, Senior, Wrestling, Political Science/Finance
• Ryan Hoffman, Sophomore, Wrestling, undeclared
• Matt Lauletti, Sophomore, Track and Field, undeclared
• Jennifer Lynch, Junior, Track and Field, Business Administration
• Amanda Martini, Senior, Track and Field, Economics and Finance
• Janelle Mentrikoski, Junior, Track and Field, Psychology
• Hannah Mitchell, Sophomore, Track and Field, Psychology
• Evan Sheppard, Senior, Track and Field, Biology
