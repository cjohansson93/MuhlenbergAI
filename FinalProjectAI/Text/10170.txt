News and Media Relations
The news & media relations team promotes news and positive stories about Muhlenberg College, its people and programs to journalists and the public. We highlight faculty, student and staff achievements; pitch compelling story ideas; connect faculty experts with journalists; handle media queries and campus visits; and develop and distribute news releases. 
For more information, contact Kristine Yahna Todaro, director of news & media relations, at kristinetodaro@muhlenberg.edu or 484-664-4343.
Follow us on Twitter to get the latest @MuhlenbergNews.
