reilly named to academic team


Muhlenberg senior Tom Reilly was named to the ESPN Academic All-District II College Division second team, as selected by the College Sports Information Directors of America.

A double major in political science and Spanish, Reilly is one of the top reserve forwards for the nationally ranked Muhlenberg mens soccer team. He has played in all 17 games, starting three, and recorded two goals and two assists.

Reilly is second among active Mule players in career points (32) and goals (12). He received All-Centennial Conference honorable mention in 2008, his first season at Muhlenberg after transferring from Temple.

Muhlenberg finished the regular season with a record of 10-1-6 and is the third seed for this weekends CC playoffs. The Mules play second-seeded Franklin & Marshall at Swarthmore on Saturday. Muhlenberg is ranked 22nd in Division III by D3soccer.com.

To be nominated for the ESPN Academic All-District team, a student-athlete must be at least a sophomore with a 3.3 cumulative grade-point average and a starter or significant reserve on his team. District II College Division consists of all NCAA Division II, NCAA Division III, NAIA and two-year colleges and universities in Delaware, the District of Columbia, Maryland, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and West Virginia.
