Muhlenberg College remembers Dr. David Amdur


	 Tuesday, June 3, 2014  04:33 PM

The College mourns the loss of Dr. David Amdur, assistant professor of economics and finance.  Our prayers and thoughts are with his family.
The link to the obituary published in the Boston Globe contains information about viewing hours and memorial service.
