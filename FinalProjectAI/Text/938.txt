Virtual Volunteering
It is important for prehealth students to engage in opportunities where they provide service to others, but in these time of the coronavirus pandemic, it may not be possible to provide this service in-person. The HPA office has found the following opportunities that may be of interest.  While this list is be no means exhaustive, it should give you a good place to start your search.











          Volunteermatch
  








Volunteermatch This website helps people match with volunteer opportunities in their area. There is a lot of opportunities on this website.






          Points of Light
  








Points of Light Offers a multitude of volunteering opportunities and the website nicely organizes the opportunities into categories to help you find the best organization that fits your needs and interests.






          Create the Good
  








Create the Good This is a searchable database of volunteer opportunities to help match volunteers to virtual gigs. To search the virtual opportunities, select âshow only home/remote opportunitiesâ. This is a great way to find opportunities for volunteering while not leaving the safety and comfort of your home. 






          The Red Cross
  








The Red Cross The Red Cross provides several volunteer positions.






          Crisis Text Line
  








Crisis Text Line This is a place that someone can volunteer to help the to offer free 24/7 support for those in crisis. The requirements are that you must be over 18 years of age and can commit to volunteering four hours each week.






          RAINN
  








RAINN The National Sexual Assault Hotline, this organization needs virtual volunteers to provide live, secure and anonymous support to victims of sexual assault and their loved ones. Volunteers will receive training and must commit to a minimum of 10 hours of service a month, for a minimum of 18 months of service. 






          The Trevor Project
  








The Trevor Project Support LGBTQ youth who are struggling with depression, suicide, coming out, and their LGBTQ identity by responding to chat messages as a crisis counselor. This volunteering opportunity requires 40-hour online training and serves one three-hour shift per week for at least a year. 






          Translators Without Borders
  








Translators Without Borders This is a website in which people who are fluent in more than one language can volunteer to help international organizations that focus on crisis relief, health, and education. 






          Tarjimly
  








Tarjimly This app connects refugees, asylees, and immigrants with people who can volunteer as translators. As a volunteer, you will receive specific notifications when someone is in need of a translator. This request can then be accepted or denied based on availability and skill requirements.
 






          Together We Rise
  








Together We Rise Together we Rise is an organization that positively helps foster children in which volunteers can help change the way children experience the foster care system. Volunteers can contribute through the use of team building and service projects. 
 






          Meals on Wheels
  








Meals on Wheels   Helps bring nutrition and meals to seniors in America who live face the threat of hunger. The most common way to volunteer is by delivering meals to homebound seniors, but there are many ways to volunteer through meals on wheels. 






          Be My Eyes
  








Be My Eyes  This app connects blind and low-vision individuals with sighted volunteers for visual assistance through live video calls. Through this app, you can answer requests from people who need help with anything varying from checking food labels, to navigating a new area. 






          Instant Wild 
  








Instant Wild   Help contribute to conservation efforts by tagging animals via images and videos. The organization will provide field guides for each of the specified projects. You can help further conserve these animals while also watching videos of these cute animals.






          Virtual Clinical Observation Program
  








Virtual Clinical Observation Program Provides pre-health students with a personal and authentic understanding of medicine from anywhere in the world. Through this course, the student will gain valuable observation experience and guidance as they continue their own educational advancement into a career as an aspiring healthcare professional. The program will feature live and pre-recorded components, standardized patients, unparalleled student engagement, and interface with healthcare professionals from UT Southwestern Medical Center. There is $30 fee to participate in the program, if the fee is a financial hardship, students could apply for a Muhlenberg Experiential Learning Grant.
