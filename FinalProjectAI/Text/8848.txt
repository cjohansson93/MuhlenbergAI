Study Lounges



Here in the Office of Residential Services, we are committed to the academic success of all students. We have set aside space in most residence halls for quiet and group study. There are study lounges in the following locations:


 


 


West Quad:


 


Prosser: Ground Floor 1st Floor 2nd Floor


 


Brown: Basement


 


Walz: 1st Floor 4th Floor


 


Taylor: 1st Floor 4th Floor


 





East Quad:


 



Martin Luther: Underground 4th Floor



 



East: 3 in Tower, 2 in East and West Corners 1st flr, 2 above breezeway 2nd and 3rd floor.



 



 The Courts: 1st Floor
 



 





South Quad:


 



Robertson / South: 2 per building - Shared between 1st and 2nd / 3rd and 4th Floors








Apartment/ Suite Style Housing:


 



The Village 1 large study space in a separate building at the top of The Village



 


2201 Chew Street Ground Floor
