CORPORATE LEADERSHIP PROGRAM BENEFITS AND DETAILS
As department managers and human resources professionals, you understand the skills needed to succeed in your organization. As experts in adult education, Muhlenberg College School of Continuing Studies knows how to design, deliver and measure the results of educational programs. We can partner to create the professional development programs you desire or work with your human resources team to provide scholarship benefits on our existing degree paths. 
 
Interested in learning more? Schedule a 30-minute needs assessment meeting with Samantha Anglestein, Corporate Outreach Manager. Or contact her at samanthaanglestein@muhlenberg.edu  or 484-664-3300.



 


  Partnership Tiers







          Tier 1 - Undergraduate Scholarships
  








Benefits from Muhlenberg Division of Graduate and Continuing Education

Single Course Scholarship to GCE Undergraduate Programs for your employees
Annual information session for your employees to promote programs

Details 

No minimum number of students required 
No contractual obligation.  Only need an MOU to enter our partner program.
Corporate Partner will send an annual email to employees regarding the scholarship opportunities available at Muhlenberg College and a link to the annual information session
Corporate Partner will promote Muhlenberg College as an employee benefit/perk as appropriate







          Tier 2 - Undergraduate & Graduate Scholarships
  








Benefits from Muhlenberg Division of Graduate and Continuing Education

Two course Undergraduate Scholarships
Single course Graduate Scholarship* (Degree only, does not apply to graduate certificate programs)
Annual information session for your employees to promote programs
Customize partnership portal on Muhlenberg.edu outlining the benefits and eligible programs

Details 

Minimum 10 student admissions per year
Presence on corporate partner's website or intranet, such as a paragraph on the partnership featuring the Muhlenberg College logo
Corporate Partner will send an annual email to employees regarding the scholarship opportunities available at Muhlenberg College and a link to the annual information session
Corporate Partner will list Muhlenberg College as an employee benefit/perk as appropriate
Opportunity to conduct in-person/virtual information sessions with employees







          Tier 3 - Cohort Scholarship Partner 

  








Benefits from Muhlenberg Division of Graduate and Continuing Education

Exclusive cohort with only your employees
Option for onsite courses at employer facility 
Annual information sessions to promote programs or a site visit to meet with prospective employees in person
Annual meeting with your human capital management leadership about your overall workforce development strategy 
Flexible cohort start date 
Partnership portal on Muhlenberg.edu outlining the benefits and eligible programs. 
Priority admissions through a program director
No application fee

Details 

Minimum 10 undergraduate students per year
Presence on a corporate partner's website or intranet, such as a paragraph on the partnership and a logo. 
Corporate Partner will send an annual email to employees regarding the scholarship opportunities available at Muhlenberg College and a link to the annual information session
Corporate partner will list Muhlenberg College as an employee benefit/perk as appropriate 
Opportunity to conduct in-person/virtual information sessions with employees
