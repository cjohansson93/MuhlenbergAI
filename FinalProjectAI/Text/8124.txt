Advisory board members bring expertise and experience to the Institute by providing institutional history, outside professional perspective, broader academic context, and student and alumni perspectives. The role of the board is to advise the director and support staff of the Institute on issues related to visioning, programming, instructional activities, promotion, and fundraising.
