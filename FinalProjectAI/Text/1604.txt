Muhlenberg College Faculty And The Civic Theatre Join To Present Film Series 

	 Tuesday, May 3, 2005  09:26 AM

The Muhlenberg College faculty and the Civic Theatre will join forces to present the Muhlenberg Film Forum. Created as part of the Civic Theatre’s 19 th Street Film series, this series will consist of post-film discussions, led by Muhlenberg College faculty. The forum schedule is as follows:
BORN INTO BROTHELSAcademy Award winner for Best Documentary FilmApril 30 - May 8
Dr. Lora Taub of the communication department will lead a discussion following the May 4 showing of the film.
DOWNFALLOscar-nominated account of Hitler's final days, starring Bruno GanzMay 7-15
Dr. Alec Marsh of the English department will lead a discussion following the May 11 showing of the film.
LOOK AT MECritically acclaimed French film about a father and daughter's efforts to reconnectMay 21-29
Dr. Thomas Cartelli of the English department will lead a discussion following the May 25 showing of the film.
All films start at 7:30 p.m., and discussions will follow. The 19th Street Theatre Film Series runs throughout April and May.  The entire schedule is available at http://www.civictheatre.com/. The discussions are part of the ticket price for that evening. For ticket information, call the Civic Theatre box office at 610-432-0888.
