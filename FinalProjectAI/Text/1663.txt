Register Now For Spring Courses At The Wescoe School 



Registration has begun for spring semester classes at The Wescoe School of Muhlenberg College. Classes begin on January 17 and will be offered on both weeknights and Saturdays.


	 Thursday, November 17, 2005  01:59 PM

Space is available in courses including: Topics in Writing & Publishing, Financial Accounting, Irish Literature, Photography I, II & III, Radio Production, Principles of Macroeconomics, Shakespeare I, Public Administration, Statistical Methods, Social Psychology, Elementary Spanish I & II, Fraud Examination, Calculus I, God Today, Leadership, Investments & Portfolios, Phenomenology, Introduction to Astronomy, Government & Politics of the Middle East, Psychology of Adolescence, Constitutional Law, Ceramics I& II, Concepts of Federal Taxation, Human Evolution, Concepts of Biology, Change as Opportunity, Theories of International Politics, The Literary Marketplace, World Geography, Topics in Environmental Science, Elementary German II, American Economic History, Drawing I & II, and more. Check the Website for a complete listing and updates.
Registration will be accepted until the first day of classes. Advisors are available to assist prospective and current students. For more information about these sessions or any of the Wescoe programs: bachelor’s and associate’s degrees, traditional or accelerated study, teacher certification, post-baccalaureate programs, financial aid opportunities, tuition reimbursement and more, please call 484-664-3300 or visit www.muhlenberg.edu/wescoe.
