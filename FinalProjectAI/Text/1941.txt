2022 Summer Study
Muhlenberg Summer Study welcomes Muhlenberg full-time traditional and Continuing Studies students, veterans and military personnel, visiting college and high school students looking to earn early college credits to enroll in up to four courses, offered in five sessions, throughout the summer (no more than two courses per session). 
With a wide range of degree credit courses online, in-person and also in a hybrid format, taught by engaging faculty, you will be a step ahead for the fall semester or a step closer to your own personal goals.
