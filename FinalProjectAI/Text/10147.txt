Senior Pride Ads
Support your favorite graduate with a Senior Pride Ad in the Ciarla yearbook!
Deadline for Senior Pride Ads: May 27, 2022
These ads are a great way to congratulate graduates and wish them luck in their future endeavors. The ads often include poems, artwork, a message from family or friends and/or photos of the student. Graduates are also invited to place an ad to memorialize their time at Muhlenberg.
Create your own personalized ad using the Balfour Online Store. It's easy! Visit Muhlenberg College Ciarla Yearbook Packages/Balfour Online Store. Within 24 hours you will receive an email directing you to Balfour.com > Log in > My Orders. The program will provide step-by-step instructions about how to create a personalized ad, including selecting and uploading photos and adding a message.
