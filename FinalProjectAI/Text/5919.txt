Volleyball Falls; Women's Cross Country, Men's Soccer Still Ranked
 Tuesday, September 13, 2011







					 




The Muhlenberg volleyball team dropped its final match before opening up Centennial Conference play, 3-0 at Richard Stockton.

Three Mules tied for the team lead in kills with 5. Freshman Alyssa Schell (left) recorded 12 digs to reach double figures for the fourth match in a row.
Click here for box score.
Click here for complete stats.

Polls: Go figure: the team that was idle last week moved up, and the team that played and won moved down.

The women’s cross country team is ranked eighth in the Mideast Region by the U.S. Track & Field and Cross Country Coaches Association. The Mules were ninth in the
preseason poll and had an impressive opening at Lehigh 11 days ago.
Click here for the complete regional rankings.

The men’s soccer team fell from No. 17 to No. 23 in the National Soccer Coaches Association of America poll despite beating Moravian in its lone game last week.
The 3-0 Mules continue to be ranked No. 3 in the Mid-Atlantic Region.
Click here for the national poll.
