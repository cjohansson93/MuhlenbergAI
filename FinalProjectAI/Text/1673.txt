Student LifeDean Of Students 












The focus of the Dean of Students organization is to encourage the personal and interpersonal growth, development, and well being of the entire student body by fostering an environment that is caring, supportive, and nurturing.
Ranging from athletics and community service, to health care and student activities, the entire organization is student focused and promotes community and social responsibility, ethics and leadership, and the values and ideals of a quality, liberal arts experience.
