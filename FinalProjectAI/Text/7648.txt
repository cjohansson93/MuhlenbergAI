Women's Tennis Fares Well at ITA Regional
 Sunday, September 21, 2014 - page 3







					 




The Muhlenberg women’s tennis team had a strong weekend in Virginia at the ITA Southeast Regional.

Sophomore Sara Finger was seeded ninth in the championship singles draw and won her first two matches before falling to the second seed
in the round of 16.

Mules advanced to the semifinals in two draws. Freshman Hannah Molk breezed through
her first two matches in blue 5 singles before dropping a close 7-6 (7-2), 5-7, 11-9 match to an opponent from
Franklin & Marshall.

In blue 2 doubles, the team of senior Sharen Resnikoff and sophomore Jamie Rosenblum
prevailed in two close matches (8-6 and 9-8) before falling to a Johns Hopkins duo.

Resnikoff (blue 2) and Rosenblum (blue 4) also won a singles match each, and Molk teamed with classmate
Molly Kowalski to reach the consolation semifinals in blue 1 doubles.

Senior Jennifer Goldberg defeated a Haverford player in the championship singles consolation.

Senior Danni Yaron (right) won a marathon in the first round at blue 7 singles, coming back
after losing the first set to win the second, 6-4, before pulling out the tiebreaker, 15-13.
