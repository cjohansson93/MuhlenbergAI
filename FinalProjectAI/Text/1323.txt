Welcome Weekend and Family Orientation



As your students move on to campus and begin their Muhlenberg College transition. You are also transitioning in many ways. Muhlenberg College offers a Family Orientation program on your move-in day, Thursday, August 25, 2022. A Family Orientation program will be offered on Sunday, August 21, 2022, for Pre-Orientation Program participants. The 2022 schedules will be available soon. Please visit the 2021 schedule for an idea of what to expect.

Pre-arrival Checklist



To successfully transition to Muhlenberg College, a list of items to complete, including links to forms and websites, has been provided below. Please note the various due dates for each item. Summer Advising Guides and Orientation Leaders will communicate these priorities to new first-year and transfer students.

Family Weekend



We are so excited to offer the opportunity to gather on campus to reconnect with the family members of Muhlenberg College students for a weekend of fun and celebration. Family Weekend will take place October 28 - 30, 2022. The 2022 schedule will be available soon. Check out the 2021 schedule for what to expect!

Parents’ Council



Members of the Parents' Council are invited annually by the president for a one-year, renewable term. They represent Muhlenberg families and are asked to share their perspectives and questions on College matters with the administration.
