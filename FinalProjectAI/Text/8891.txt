Assistant Dean of Students/Judicial Officer
Background A 2002 graduate of Muhlenberg College, Ms. Schubert has made an incredible impact on the student body and is well versed on the inner workings of the Dean of Students organization.
Ms. Schubert has been Muhlenberg's Judicial Officer since 1999. In this capacity, her primary responsibility is working closely with Campus Safety and Residential Services in determining disciplinary actions stemming from College Student Code of Conduct Violations. Ms. Schubert oversees the training of the Judicial Committees and Hearing Boards, and is a liaison with the Appeals Board.
In 2006, Ms. Schubert was promoted to Assistant Dean of Students.
In addition to her Dean of Students responsibilities, she is also the advisor to the College's dance team, "The Perkulators," and the a cappella group, "The Dynamics."
