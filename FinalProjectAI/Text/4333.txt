“MV-Pete” lives up to nameat cc track championships








Muhlenberg junior Peter Rice was named Most Outstanding Performer for track events at the Centennial Conference Indoor Track & Field
Championships after winning three gold medals (in the 200, 400 and 4x400) and narrowly missing a fourth in the 4x200. Rice became the third Mule male athlete, following 
Will Elson (2003) and David Chorney (2009), to be named Most Outstanding, and tied Elson and Lex Mercado (2006) for the most golds at the CC indoor meet.

Freshman Erin Saucier took first place in the pole vault for the women.

The Muhlenberg men finished third for the second straight year, and the women came in seventh.
