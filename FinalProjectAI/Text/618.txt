Business Administration with a concentration in Healthcare Management (BBA)
Healthcare is one of the fastest-growing industries in the United States. The business needs of a healthcare organization are unique, and a run-of-the-mill Business Administration degree only offers some of the knowledge and skills needed. 
Accelerated Career Growth with an Accelerated Degree
Developed in partnership with a corporate advisory board consisting of regional healthcare providers, our healthcare specialization track focuses on practical applications and career-oriented skills. Classes are taught by faculty with advanced degrees and direct industry experience. 
Finish in as little as 22-months with our accelerated programs.

Consists of 16 five-week courses, meeting only once a week 
Hybrid online/in-person delivery for flexibility  
Class costs include books and materials; No hidden fees
Fixed tuition will never increase for the duration of your program
Generous transfer policies, including credit for prior learning

Learn essential career skills

Courses aligned to the specific challenges of the health services industry
Project-based learning
Practical applications that are relevant to day-to-day work
Real-life projects, work with select non-profit organizations
Focus on the development of soft skills you'll need in professional environments
