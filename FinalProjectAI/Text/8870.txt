Kenneth Ryder, MDMedical Doctor
BackgroundDr. Ryder, a Muhlenberg College graduate, has been with the Muhlenberg College Health Center since 2017.  He is certified by the American Board of Family Practice.  He received his medical training from the Pennsylvania State University College of Medicine .  His family practice office is in Schneckville, PA.  He provides physician services on select Thursdays in the Health Center.
