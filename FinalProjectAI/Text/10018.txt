 John Pearce RetiredAssociate Professor - French, Spanish(610)438-2478

Background B.A., M.A. University of Michigan Ph.D., University of Chicago
Dr. John Pearce was a member of the 'Berg faculty from 1970 to 2003. He taught pre-Revolution French literature, Business French and Business Spanish, along with language courses for the all-college language requirement. He was director of the Mellon Grant Project: developing multimedia, interactive software for language learning.
Dr. Pearce was editor of the PA State Modern Language Assn. Newsletter from 1990-2003. He was also editor of the departmental newsletter. Dr. Pearce is active in the community with his church, Meals on Wheels, and NAMI (National Alliance for the Mentally Ill).
