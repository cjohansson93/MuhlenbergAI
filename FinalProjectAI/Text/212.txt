Department of Sociology and Anthropology Institutional Review Policies & Procedures
Please see the Departmental Institutional Review Procedures to determine which level of review your proposal qualifies for: exempt, expedited, or full review." For full review you must submit the proposal to the college IRB. For exempt and expedited review please submit your IRB to the department IRB representatives. Please use the appropriate forms and guidelines on the college IRB website.
