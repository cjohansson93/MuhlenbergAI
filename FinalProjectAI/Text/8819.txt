Printing for Faculty and Staff
Print management via PaperCut is available for departments. Faculty and staff may print from College issued computers, as well as mobile devices connected to the MuhlenbergWiFi network.
Copy and scan management is also available via PaperCut. Faxing capability is available on select devices. 
Want to check your Papercut account to confirm jobs pending release, cancel a job, etc...? Visit the Papercut website to login to your account. 
Having issues printing?
If you encounter issues printing, copying or scanning via PaperCut, please report the issue via the IT Support Portal.
