Men's Golf 10th at Allegheny
 Monday, September 17, 2012







					 




The Muhlenberg men’s golf team finished 10th at its first two-day tournament of the year, Allegheny’s Guy W. Kuhn Invitational.

Senior Brandon Davis (left) led the Mules with a 9-over-par 153 over the 36 holes. He shot a 74 in the first round and a 79 in the second. 
Davis played in the Kuhn tournament in three of his four years and shot in the 70s in all six rounds.

Also shooting in the 70s both days was junior Chris DiMaio, who came in with a 156.
