The data table will have the number of caterpillars that were found broken down by location, site (transect), and plant #. This will be done for every day that beat sheeting was done. This will potentially tell us the dates where the populations were highest, the habitat where the populations were the highest and even on what plant they were the highest. We will also be able to tell if there is a difference between locations of Raker and Graver.
