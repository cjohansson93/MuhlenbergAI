Register Now For Muhlenberg Summer Courses

	 Tuesday, March 22, 2005  10:06 AM

Muhlenberg College will offer over 80 summer study courses this year over two sessions. A wide selection of four-, six- and ten-week courses are available. Summer I begins May 23. Session II begins July 6. Registrations are now being accepted, and will be accepted until the first day of classes for each session. 
Summer study provides opportunities for high school students to get a head start on their college education, for traditional students from other institutions and adult students to move closer to their degrees and for community members to pursue their interests. Courses will be available in the following disciplines: accounting, anthropology, art history, Asian traditions, biology, business administration, chemistry, classical civilization, economics, English, environmental science, history, mathematical sciences, music, philosophy, physics, political science, psychology, religion, Spanish and studio arts.
For more information or to receive a catalog, call 484-664-3300 or visit Muhlenberg Summer Study online at www.muhlenberg.edu/mgt/summer.
