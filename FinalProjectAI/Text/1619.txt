Fall Courses Available At The Wescoe School 



The Wescoe School of Muhlenberg College will offer over 80 liberal arts courses this fall. Registrations are now being accepted. Classes in Session I will begin August 29.


	 Tuesday, July 26, 2005  01:59 PM

Session II classes start the week of October 24. Fall sessions in the Accelerated Degree program will begin in mid-October.
Space is still available in courses including: Corporation Finance, Military History of WWII, Marketing Research, History: American "Bad Guys", Classical Mythology, Being and Knowing, Business Law I, Buddhism from East to West, Management Policy and Strategy, Introduction to Astronomy, Accounting Information Systems, Gothic & Horror in Our Culture, Business Ethics, Mergers & Acquisitions, Photography I, American Writers, Organizational Communication, Special Topics: The Bible, Vodou and Zombification, Religions of China, Film History, and others.
Registrations will be accepted until the first day of classes. For information about these sessions, financial aid, transfer of credit or to set up an individual counseling session, please call 484-664-3300 or visit www.muhlenberg.edu/wescoe.
