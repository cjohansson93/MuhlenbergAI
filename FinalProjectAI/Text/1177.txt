Employee Discounts
Employees of Muhlenberg College can take advantage of discounts offered by Muhlenberg College preferred contract suppliers. These suppliers have agreed to offer discounts to faculty and staff. Personal purchases will include sales tax and may include shipping charges.
The Purchasing Department does not endorse any specific supplier or specific equipment; purchases made through these programs are between the individual and the supplier. Muhlenberg College is not liable for any personal purchases or agreements between employees and suppliers listed on this web page.
To access the suppliers please login to the Staff Tools through the OneLogin application portal and click on the Purchasing Contracts. You may then select Personal Use
