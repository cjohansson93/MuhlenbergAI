Academic Advantage
Learning Environments
Scholarship isn't limited to the classroom. Our labs, performance areas and collaborative spaces are designed for and structured around integrative learning.







Baker Center for the Arts



The Baker Center for the Arts houses the Art, English, Music, Theatre and Dance Departments and classroom, office and performance/rehearsal spaces.




Learn More
















Ettinger Hall



Ettinger houses the Accounting, Business, Economics & Finance; History; Languages, Literatures & Cultures; and Political Science Departments as well as IT.




Learn More
















Martin Art Gallery



Martin Art Gallery, accessible via the Baker Center for the Arts, hosts multiple exhibitions in the main Gallery and the Galleria area.




Learn More
















Moyer Hall



Moyer houses the Education, Philosophy, Psychology and Religion Studies Departments, the Institute for Jewish Christian Understanding and classrooms and labs.




Learn More
















New Science & Shankweiler Buildings



New Science and Shankweiler house the Biology and Neuroscience Departments, the Acopian Center for Ornithology and labs, classrooms and research spaces.




Learn More
















Trexler Library



Trexler Library hosts a wide variety of programs, discussions and speakers throughout the year and provides access to 310,000 volumes and 29,000 journals.




Learn More
















Trumbower Science Building



Trumbower houses the Chemistry, Mathematics & Computer Science and Physics Departments and labs, lecture halls, classrooms and research spaces.




Learn More
