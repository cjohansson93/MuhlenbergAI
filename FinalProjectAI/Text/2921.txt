cohn named toall-region team


Senior Tara Cohn of the Muhlenberg field hockey team was selected to the Longstreth/National Field Hockey Coaches Association All-South Region second team.

A first-team All-Centennial Conference pick, Cohn led the Mules with nine goals and tied for the team led with three assists. 
She finished eighth in the CC in points per game (1.40) and sixth in goals per game (0.60).  

For her career, Cohn was sixth in program history with 13 assists and 13th with 57 points, accumulated mostly in her last two seasons.

Cohn is the first Muhlenberg player to make the all-region team since Lauren Rutt in 2007. Five different Mules have received all-region honors a total of seven times since 2000.
