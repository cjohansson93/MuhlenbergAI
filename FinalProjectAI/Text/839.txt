First-Rate Facilities 
All majors have 24 hour access to our production and post-production facilities featuring a multimedia TV studio with video control room, a classroom with 16 iMacs, all with an array of multimedia editing software including Creative Cloud, two post-production labs, a mobile gaming lab, and an FM studio. 
Check availability and reserve a room
State-of-the-Art Equipment
Majors have immediate access, even as first-years to up-to-date and top-of-the-line production equipment including professional digital video cameras, digital audio recorders, a variety of directional and omnidirectional microphones, lighting kits and tripod systems, and various production accessories.
