Young Wrestling Team Opens at King's
 Saturday, November 1, 2014 - page 4







					 





The Muhlenberg wrestling team opened its season at the King’s Monarch Invitational.

Of the seven Mules who competed, six were freshmen getting their first taste of collegiate wrestling, and most had 
close, competitive matches.

Freshman Ben Peck (left) picked up two one-point wins at 133, one in the first match of his
career.
