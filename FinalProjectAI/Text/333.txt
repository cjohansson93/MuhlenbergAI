Home Mortgage

Originally adopted in 2012, the Home Mortgage Incentive program was designed to encourage employee home ownership in the Allentown School district. Muhlenberg College will contribute $1,500 toward the expenses of purchasing a primary residence in the Allentown School District. A maxium of five stipends are available per year to regular, full-time employees. Eligible employees must not currently own a residential home in the Allentown School District. This program is funded for 2020-2021, and an annual review of the program's success will determine its continuation in future years. Please use the Stipend Application to apply for the stipend.
