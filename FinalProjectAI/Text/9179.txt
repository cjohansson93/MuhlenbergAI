In Memoriam: Dr. Donald W. Shive


Muhlenberg remembers an esteemed professor and treasured community member.


	 Thursday, January 18, 2018  08:15 AM

Dr. Donald Shive, a beloved chemistry professor of 46 years at Muhlenberg College, passed away on Wednesday, December 20, 2017. We invite the Muhlenberg College community to join in a Celebration of Life service in Egner Chapel on Saturday, January 20, 2018 at 2:00 p.m.
Dr. Shive's obituary and memorial contribution information is available on Legacy.com.
