On Friday, September 17 at 7:00 pm ET, Muhlenberg will shine a spotlight on the philanthropic support of the Henry Melchoir Muhlenberg Society, celebrate the accomplishments of the Alumni Achievement Award recipients and welcome the members of the Classes of 1970 and 1971 (who are celebrating their 50th class reunions).
This is an invitation-only event.
