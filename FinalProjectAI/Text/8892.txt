Michele Paules LCSW Director of Student Support Services
Background:
Michele assists students within moving beyond emotional and behavioral concerns so that optimal connections can be found.  This support is not counseling or therapy.  Michele works with students through a combination of advocacy, strategy and skill building, as well as, seeking connections and resources within the campus community to assist students to be at their best.
