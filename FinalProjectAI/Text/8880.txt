Mission Statement
Muhlenberg College Health and Counseling Services delivers short-term health and counseling care that is student-centered, multiculturally aware, confidential and of the highest quality. 

 Philosophy
Health Services exists to provide health and wellness services to the students of Muhlenberg College. Nurses and physicians work together as advocates with students, assisting them in meeting their health care needs. Health Services strives to serve as facilitators for students to become educated recipients of health care. Health Services is dedicated to assisting students in their acquisition of the knowledge, skills, behaviors, and attitudes necessary to become self-directed in pursuit of their intellectual development.  Health Services is committed to providing services that are equitable and inclusive.
