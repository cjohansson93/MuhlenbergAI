Summer Intern Housing is now sold-out for Summer 2022. We are no longer accepting applications. Thank you!

Muhlenberg College 2022 Summer Intern Housing Program will commence on Friday, May 27th, 2022.Online registration for Summer 2022 will be available here in early 2022. 

2022
Summer Corporate Internship
Housing available at Robertson, South, The Village and 2201 Chew Street
Muhlenberg College
The housing offered at Robertson, South, The Village and 2201 Chew Street includes luxury, apartment-style living in a picturesque, wooded, park like setting. Each apartment is fully furnished and the kitchen is equipped with a full set of appliances. 
The kitchen in Robertson and South contains a refrigerator, microwave and sink. There are two large recreational rooms with full kitchens, a television and a second floor mezzanine area. 
Housing is offered at a rate of $24.00 per day.  This entitles you to a private bedroom within an apartment-style suite.  The remainder of the suite is shared space.
 
Note:  If occupants move-in or move-out on any other day than the 1st or last day of the month, monthly rate will be pro-rated based on the number of days within that month.  


Application Information:
Interns must complete and submit an application. Once the application has been reviewed and accepted by Muhlenberg College, a lease agreement will be forwarded to you with additional payment and move-in instructions.
The lease can be signed at check-in, but applications must be submitted by April 15, 2022. Preference will be given to applications received by April 1, 2022.
Final Application Due Date: May 1, 2022
Late applications may take up to 2 weeks to process
* Please note: Until further notice, we are not accepting any applications which are less than a 60-night consecutive stay.
 
Intern Housing Information:
All housing for Summer 2021 Interns will be located in one of the following dorms: Robertson, South, The Village or 2201 Chew Street. All dorms are non-smoking.
2201 Chew Street suites can house up to 4 interns (2 doubles). Robertson, South and The Village suites can house up to 4 interns all in separate bedrooms.
 
Assignment Information:
If there are fewer than 4 applications from an intern program, we reserve the right to house interns from different programs together. Suites may be co-ed, but only if all interns in the suite approve.
Roommate/Suitemate requests will be honored only if both request each other.
If any applicants feel that they may need specific housing accommodations, please contact JoEllen Rooney at 484-664-3810 (or via email joellenrooney@muhlenberg.edu) to discuss arrangements or accommodations.
