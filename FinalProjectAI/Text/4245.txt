Twenty-one Muhlenberg College student-athletes recently were recognized for their performance in the classroom and on the field with their selection to the Centennial Conference Academic Honor Roll for the winter ’11 season.

To be named to the Academic Honor Roll, a student must be a sophomore or higher in class standing; a starter or key reserve on his or her team; and carry at least a 3.40 cumulative grade-point average.

Following is an alphabetical list of the Muhlenberg students who were honored, with their class year, sport and major(s). Mule seniors who made the Academic Honor Roll each of the last three winter seasons are listed in bold:

• Michael Baer, Junior, Track and Field, Biochemistry
• Amanda Brennan, Sophomore, Track and Field, Neuroscience
• Steve Cavanaugh, Senior, Basketball, Business Administration
