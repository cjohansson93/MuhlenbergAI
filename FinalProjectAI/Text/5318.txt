chorney named athlete of the week


Muhlenberg senior David Chorney was named indoor track and field athlete of the week by the Centennial Conference.

Chorney captured the 3,000 meters at Lehigh’s Covert Classic on Saturday, breaking his own school record by nearly five seconds to set a new standard of 8:40.15.
Chorney won by more than eight seconds in a field of 40 runners, all but nine from Division I or Division II schools.

His time is the best in the 3,000 meters in the Centennial Conference so far this season.

Chorney is the third straight member of the Muhlenberg men’s team to earn the weekly honor, following Stuart Udis and Brendan Kirk.
