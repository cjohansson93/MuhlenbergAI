Muhlenberg College Announces Faculty Promotions



Muhlenberg College is pleased to announce the following faculty promotions, approved at the spring meeting of the Board of Trustees.


	 Monday, May 2, 2005  09:41 AM

Muhlenberg College is pleased to announce the following faculty promotions, approved at the spring meeting of the Board of Trustees.
Lawrence Hass has been promoted to professor of philosophy. Hass earned his B.A. at Ripon College and his M.A. and Ph.D. in philosophy at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He joined the Muhlenberg faculty in 1991.
Holmes Miller has been promoted to professor of business. He earned his B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. from Northwestern University. He joined the Muhlenberg faculty in 1991.
Arthur Raymond has been promoted to professor of economics. He earned his B.S. from Bryant College and his M.A. and Ph.D. from Tufts University. He joined the Muhlenberg faculty in 1991.
Founded in 1848, Muhlenberg College is a highly selective liberal arts college in Allentown, Pa.
