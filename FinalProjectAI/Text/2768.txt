Two Close with Losses at Gettysburg


Field Hockey

The Mules ended their season with a 6-1 loss at 17th-ranked Gettysburg. Sophomore Chessie Martinez scored the
lone goal, and senior Michelle Consiglio made 13 saves – the second-highest total of her career – in her last game.

Volleyball

Muhlenberg closed out the 2008 season with a 3-1 loss to CC regular-season co-champion Gettysburg. Senior Megan Eiser recorded
32 assists, five kills and three blocks in her last career match, while classmate Danielle Losonci bowed out with a 10-dig effort.

The  men blanked Ursinus, 3-0, in their last regular-season game. The fifth-place Mules (12-5-1, 5-3-1) will travel to Dickinson on Wednesday for a first-round playoff game. Gettysburg, Johns Hopkins and Dickinson all tied for second with 17 points (one more than Muhlenberg), with Dickinson getting the fourth seed on tiebreakers.

Freshman Cody Antonini converted a free kick from junior Samuel Hayden midway through the first half to open the scoring against the Bears (7-11, 3-6). Senior Billy Dalton netted his seventh goal of the season shortly thereafter.

The Mules were the beneficiary of an own goal late in the game. Senior Joseph Gogel made a great save in the final five minutes to preserve the shutout.

Muhlenberg is 15-0-1 all-time against Ursinus in CC games.
