We encourage and welcome you to visit our campus and meet with our VP Executive Director, Dean of Graduate Studies, or other graduate admissions advisors.  Visiting prior to application is not necessary. However, it can be a chance to explore the fit between you and Muhlenberg College School of Graduate Studies.  It can also be a chance to develop a relationship with a graduate advisor who can then be a resource for you. 
Admission Criteria
Muhlenberg College admits applicants whose profile (a combination of prior academic performance, career accomplishments, community engagement through volunteerism, and other leadership activities) demonstrates the greatest potential for graduate study and contribution to both their profession and to a diverse society. 
Note that undergraduate Muhlenberg students who wish to apply will need to have secured before the end of their junior year:

Approval of their academic advisor
Approval of the Dean of Graduate Studies

Financial hardship should not be a barrier to enrollment. Please contact an advisor to discuss options.
