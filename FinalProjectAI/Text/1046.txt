Judy Kuncio  Office Manager

BackgroundJudy joined the Office of Residential Services during the summer of 2017 after working in the Office of Admission as an Administrative Assistant for ten years. Judy coordinates with the rest of the Office's professional staff in order to prepare the weekly RA Duty Schedule, keep the supply inventory current, assist in key management, and take care of scheduling all appointments for the Office. Judy is also the Office's liason to the outside companies that partner with Muhlenberg.
