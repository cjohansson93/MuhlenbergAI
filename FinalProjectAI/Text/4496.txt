deangelis named toall-cc team


Junior back Brittany DeAngelis was the lone Muhlenberg representative on the All-Centennial Conference field hockey team.

DeAngelis started all 16 games for the Mules and tied for third in the CC with 6 defensive saves. She made a huge play in Muhlenberg's first win of the season,
hustling to keep a ball out of the cage and preserving a 2-1 lead at McDaniel. 

For her career, DeAngelis has 11 defensive saves to rank fifth on the Mules' all-time list.
