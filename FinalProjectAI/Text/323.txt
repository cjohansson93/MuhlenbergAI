Employee Spring Fling - Schedule of Events
Check-in starts at 11:30am in Parents Plaza at the Employee Wellness tables. 
Activities will run throughout the event from 11:30am-2:00pm on the College Green. Winners of the Employee Walking Challenge will be announced at the event*. 
A buffet lunch will be served in Parents Plaza from 11:30am-2:00pm (ticket required). 
Vendors will be available throughout the event. 
Stop by the Employee Wellness table (Parents Plaza) to fill out an exit survey* before you leave!
