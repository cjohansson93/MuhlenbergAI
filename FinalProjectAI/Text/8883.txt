FAQs
Q: How do I become a member of PHAM?
A: We accept new applicants at the end of the fall semester.  There is a written application and interview process.  Applicants will be notified via email. Also see the “Get Involved” page.
Q: Where is Peer Health Advocates at Muhlenberg located?
A: PHAM is a student-led organization run through the Health Center.  Programs are campus-wide, or held in resident halls as requested by RAs.  If you are interested in requesting a program please email either Meghan Gill or Anna Martens.
Q: How do I find out about programs and events by Peer Health Advocates at Muhlenberg?
A:  PHAM advertises on campus through the ‘Berg Bulletin, flyers, banners, and in resident halls. Stay connected with us on Facebook!
