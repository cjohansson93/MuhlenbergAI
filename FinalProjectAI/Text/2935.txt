 Remembering Tom Grace


TWO FIELD HOCKEY SENIORS NAMED ALL-CC
 



Two members of the Muhlenberg field hockey team were selected to the All-Centennial Conference team.

Senior Tara Cohn (left) was named to the first team a year after being a second teamer. Cohn led the Mules with nine goals and tied for the team led with three assists. She finished eighth in the CC in points per game (1.40) and sixth in goals per game (0.60).

Senior Nicki Notarianni received honorable mention for the second time in her career. Notarianni’s seven goals were second on the Mules, as were her 16 points. She led Muhlenberg with four game-winning goals.

Both seniors end their careers near the top of Muhlenberg’s all-time scoring list. Notarianni’s 72 career points put her sixth on the all-time list, while Cohn’s 57 place her 13th. Notarianni ranks sixth in career goals (32), and Cohn is sixth in career assists (13).
