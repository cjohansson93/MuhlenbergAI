          What is Google Workspace
  








Google Workspace (formerly GSuite for Education) is Muhlenberg's Communication and Collaboration solution of best-in-class productivity tools built for teaching and learning.






          Core Apps
  








G Suite Core Services are Gmail, Calendar, Chrome Sync, Classroom, Contacts, Drive, Docs, Forms, Groups, Sheets, Sites, Slides, Chat, Meet, Hangouts and Vault.  
Learn more about the Core Apps supported by the Office of Information Technology.
