The Writing Minor requires 6 courses, as follows:

Introductory Courses in Writing: Students must complete at least two 200-level courses in writing.
Advanced Writing Courses: Students must complete at least two courses at the 300-level or above. All of these courses have prerequisites.
Elective in Writing: Students must complete one additional course in writing, at any level.
Living Writers or another 300-level of higher course in literature: students must complete one course.

200 or 300-level Special Topics may substitute for the above requirements. Please contact the Director of the Writing Minor, Linda Miller.
For information on current course offerings, please visit the Course Catalog search under Writing Minor.
