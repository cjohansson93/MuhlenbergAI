Seegers Union Mission Statement

The J. Conrad & Hazel J. Seegers Union provides a hub for integration of the tools for teaching and learning and a venue for educating the whole student.  Seegers Union supports the campus in the development of individuals and community through education, socialization, advocacy, and the delivery of services.
