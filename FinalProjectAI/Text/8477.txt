Student Organizations:

Rejoice - the Muhlenberg College Gospel Choir, is a spirit-filled musical ensemble. Comprised of students from various places, races and creeds, the Gospel Choir is truly reflective of the diverse interests and ethnicities within the Muhlenberg College community. Their common bond is their love of music and a passion to praise God through song.
Disciplemakers - DiscipleMakers Christian Fellowship is a student group at Muhlenberg College that aims to help students learn more about the person of Jesus, grow deeper in their relationship with God, and be equipped to live out their faith during the college years..
Muslim Students Association - The object and purpose of the Muslim Students Association is to provide common space for Muslim Muhlenberg students and to spread Islamic awareness across campus. For more information, please contact the Chaplain's Office (chaplain@muhlenberg.edu).
