Technology to Borrow



Library Home|Borrowing|Technology to Borrow






Trexler Library recognizes the complex and diverse needs of its patrons and is committed to acquiring the necessary materials to match those needs. The materials listed below can be checked out from the Lending Services desk on A-Level. 
Cell Phone Chargers
The Lending Services Desk has iPhone chargers (pre-iPhone 4 and post-iPhone 5), Type C USB chargers, and Android/Micro-USB chargers available for checkout. These items come with the charging cable and an outlet cube.
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Note: A phone charging station is located behind the Information Services desk that is available for use. This station is not available for check and stays behind the Information Services desk.
Chromebooks
Four Chromebooks are available for checkout. These computers are 'frozen', so you cannot do save personal files to the hard drive. Personal files will not be recovered after reboot. 
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
External Optic Drives (Blu-ray and DVD)
Many newer generation laptops no longer include a disc drive. Blu-ray and DVD external disc drives are both Mac and Windows compatible devices to allow patrons to view AV discs on their devices. These drives are not Chromebook compatible.  
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Headphones
Headphones are available for check out and the Lending Services desk. These headphones can be used for personal devices as well as Trexler Library computers and A/V viewing stations. Patrons can also borrow headphone splitters in the event a group wishes to use a viewing station together.
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Laptop Security Cables
Trexler Library encourages patrons to keep their items secure so laptop security cables are available for checkout from the Lending Services desk. These cables are carbon-strengthened steel that can lock a patron's laptop to most tables in Trexler Library. 
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Presentation Remotes
Wireless presentation remote control with built-in red laser pointer has up to 100-foot wireless range, intuitive touch-keys for easy slideshow control, 2.4 GHz wireless USB receiver stores inside the remote for easy pack-up. These remotes are excellent for in-library presentations or practice. 
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Webcams
Webcams are well suited for 1080p widescreen video calling and recording. Our Logitech webcams are equipped with two microphones to capture natural stereo audio as well as autofocus to deliver clear images in consistent high definition. They work with Windows, Mac, Chrome OS, and Android. 
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
Wireless Keyboards
Wireless keyboards are equipped with a USB receiver allowing the keyboard to connect to any computer. 
Checkout period: 4 hours, in-library use only
Fine: Fines of $0.25 per hour (or portion of an hour) will be accrued for late returns. Please plan accordingly.
