Muhlenberg to Host Coronavirus Panel



The event, "The Coronavirus Pandemic: COVID-19," takes place on Tuesday, March 10. It is free and open to the public. 


	 Wednesday, March 4, 2020  03:17 PM


Join five Muhlenberg College experts for a discussion of this important and current issue, examined from multiple disciplinary perspectives. The event will take place Tuesday, March 10, at 6 p.m. in Miller Forum, Moyer Hall. It's free and open to the public, and refreshments will be served. A Q&A session will follow the panel discussion. The entire evening will be available via live-stream here.
The panel will include:

Chrysan Cronin, director and assistant professor of public health
Rachel Hamelers, teaching and learning librarian
Kathleen Bachynski, assistant professor of public health
Casey James Miller, assistant professor of anthropology
Lufei Teng, assistant professor of economics and finance

You can find more information on coronavirus and the College's response to it here.
