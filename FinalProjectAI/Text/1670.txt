Full Calendar
Masses in Egner Chapel are open to the public in accord with the College's current visitor guidelines for the COVID-19 pandemic. All visitors are asked to comply with these guidelines if they desire to attend Mass in Egner Chapel. Students who attend Mass in either Egner Chapel or the Newman Center are asked to follow all current College policies on masking and social distancing. 
 
Celebration of Mass and the Sacrament of Reconciliation
Masses are usually celebrated on Sunday Evenings at 9:00 PM in Egner Chapel when students are not on break.  The Sacrament of Reconciliation is offered for Muhlenberg and Cedar Crest Colleges communities 8:30 PM prior to the beginning of Mass on Sundays or at other times by appointment.
Daily Masses are generally celebrated Mondays and Thursdays in St. Patrick Chapel on the second floor of the Newman Center. Mass on Monday is in English and offered according to the Ordinary Form of the Roman Rite. Mass on Thursday is in Latin and offered according to the Extraordinary Form of the Roman Rite.  
For up to date information regarding the Mass Schedule please see the full calendar of activities linked above, or visit our Facebook page.
Social Activities
Catholic Campus Ministry engages in many social activities from community fellowship dinners to joint activities with Chapel and Hillel.  Muhlenberg College students are invited to visit our social media feeds for up to date activity information.
