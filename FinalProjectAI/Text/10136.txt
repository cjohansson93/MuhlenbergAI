Announcements
Customized graduation announcements are available to order online at Herff Jones.  
Bookstore
The Bookstore is open Monday through Friday, 8:30 am to 4:30 pm. Saturday hours will begin in February. Online ordering is available through the Bookstore website.
How can I view/purchase a video of the Commencement Ceremony?
For those who cannot be in attendance, a livestream viewing of Baccalaureate and Commencement will begin 15 minutes prior to each ceremony. The links will be available on this website.
Highlights
A free download will be available on the Commencement website. Footage will include both Baccalaureate and Commencement. 
Roses
Roses will be available for pre-purchase from the Crime Victims Council of Allentown and will be available for pickup at the PPL Center in the Main WFMZ Lobby between 9:00 and 10:00 a.m. Order roses online or by slecting the printable Muhlenberg College form at the same link. Orders must be recieved by Monday, May 16th. 2022. 
Photography - Register Online: School PIN# 540020
A professional photographer from Island Photography will be taking photographs of each graduate as they receive their diploma. Please register with Island Photography for information regarding photo coverage of Commencement and to receive the graduate's proofs. They can be reached by phone at 516-767-1234.
