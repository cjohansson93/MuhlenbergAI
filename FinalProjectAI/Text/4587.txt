The Muhlenberg field hockey team took to the road for the first time this season and dropped a 4-0 decision at Drew.

A pair of Muhlenberg teams are moving on up in national and regional rankings.





Muhlenberg returns to action Saturday at the Dickinson Long/Short Invitational.



The Mule mens soccer team (3-0-1) jumped four spots to No. 21 in the latest edition of the D3soccer.com poll, released Tuesday. Muhlenberg is one of three Centennial Conference teams in the top 25, joined by No. 4 Johns Hopkins and No. 11 Swarthmore.
New York University, which tied Muhlenberg 1-1 Saturday afternoon, was named as a team receiving votes.

The Mules also maintained their No. 4 ranking in the Mid-Atlantic region according to the National Soccer Coaches Association of America. Muhlenberg is tied with CC-foe Dickinson for the fourth spot, while Johns Hopkins (No. 1), Swarthmore (No. 2) and Messiah (No. 3) round out the top five. Future Mule opponents DeSales and Drew check in at eighth and ninth in the region, respectively.
