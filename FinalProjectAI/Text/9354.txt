Information Session at The Wescoe School of Muhlenberg College



The Wescoe School of Muhlenberg College will host an information session for prospective students. 


	 Thursday, October 28, 2010  11:45 AM

It will be held on Wednesday, November 3 from 3:00 to 7:00 p.m. in the Gabriel House across from the Trexler Library on the Muhlenberg College campus in Allentown.
Information about bachelor’s degrees in traditional and accelerated formats, military education benefits, Teacher Certification, associate’s degrees, and major certificates will be available. All attending will receive an application fee waiver and enjoy refreshments and informal conversation with Wescoe school staff. An optional breakout session on Teacher Certification will be held at both 4:00 and 6:00 p.m.
Guests will also have the opportunity to complete a complimentary career/skills assessment, sign up to sit in on a future class and register for review options for upcoming classes. For more information or driving directions, please call 484-664-3300, email wescoe@muhlenberg.edu or visit http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/wescoe.
