Contact the Office of Global Education

It is MANDATORY that you contact the Office of Global Education office upon your arrival. The Office of Global Education needs your mailing address, phone and fax numbers immediately. You MUST USE your Muhlenberg email address while abroad. In fact, effective immediately, all email correspondence with the Office of Global Education will be via your Muhlenberg College email only. You can contact the Office of Global Education through:

Email: studyabroad@muhlenberg.edu
Fax (remember to add country code 001) - 484-664-3840
Mailing address: Muhlenberg College, Office of Global Education, 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, PA 18104 USA

The purpose of this information is so that our office may notify the indicated Muhlenberg College offices including the following:


Housing (if we do not notify housing, you will not be included in the lottery nor be sent applications for MILE housing or off-campus housing)


Registration (if we do not notify the Registrarâs Office, you will not be informed by the Registrarâs Office of the registration procedures for the following semester)


Office of Global Education, (in addition to the above, if there are changes to your schedule we can request the necessary course approval)
