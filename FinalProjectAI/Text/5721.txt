Seven Muhlenberg athletes recently were named to the fall Centennial Conference Sportsmanship Team.

Each member institution was permitted to nominate one student from each fall program who exemplifies the best in sportsmanship. 
Nominations were submitted by the athletic departments and head coaches.

The Mules honored and their sports are:

• Janette Adams, junior, women’s cross country
• Joseph Brancale, senior, men’s cross country
• Lauren Rutt, senior, field hockey
• Jim Jeffers, junior, football
• Jayne Condon, sophomore, women’s soccer
• Peter Bennett, senior, men’s soccer
• Megan Young, senior, volleyball
