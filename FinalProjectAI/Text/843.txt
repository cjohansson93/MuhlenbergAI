Walson 115
Along with the classic lecture-style podium, tech-wall and projector, 115 is host to sixteen iMac computers surrounding the periphery. Illustrating our diverse use of the room, each computer is equipped with software for basic and advanced movie-making, image editing, scriptwriting and music creation (including Creative Cloud).
