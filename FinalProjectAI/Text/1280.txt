Mission Statement
The Muhlenberg College Mailroom endeavors to:

Process all incoming and outgoing mail in a timely, reliable, confidential manner;
Provide courteous, efficient, accurate customer service to all faculty, staff, students;
Adopt cost-effective measures where appropriate; and
Adhere to policies and procedures established by the United States Postal Service.

Location
The Mailroom is located in the General Services Building behind the Haas College Center. The General Services Building is Building #22 on the Muhlenberg campus map.

Hours of Operation (Monday-Friday; closed Saturdays-Sundays)
Fall/Spring - 8:30 a.m.-4:30 p.m.
Summer - 8:30 a.m.-4:15 p.m.
