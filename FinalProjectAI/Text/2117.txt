Student Application for Summer Research Funding through the Biology Department or Neuroscience Program
APPLICATION DEADLINE:January 28, 2022
1) Provide project title, your name, and class year.
2) In fewer than 300 words, please describe the summer research project in which you will engage, including a brief theoretical context.
3) How would you identify your field of research (e.g. conservation biology, developmental biology, ecology, epidemiology, molecular and cellular biology, neuroscience)?  Which of the these awards described do you think is most suitable?
4) In fewer than 100 words, please describe previous experiences that have prepared you to successfully complete this summerâs research.5) In fewer than 100 words, please describe your future academic or career goals and how this summer experience is likely to encourage those goals.6) In fewer than 100 words, please describe your past participation within the community of the Biology Department, Neuroscience program, Muhlenberg campus, or local community (no more than four highlighted activities).7) Do you have any other obligations (e.g. classes, other work, internships) that may affect your ability to work full time (40hrs/week) on this project? If so, please describe.
8) The deadline for submission of the application is Monday, January 28, 2022 at 4:00 pm. You must submit your application in PDF format WITH YOUR SURNAME AS PART OF THE FILE NAME via email to Dr. Marten Edwards, Chair of Biology.Please CC yourself on the email so you can be sure the email was received.
9) Your faculty mentor (the person in whose lab you will be working) needs to answer a series of questions as well. Please forward the link below to your mentor and double-check to be sure that s/he has submitted the application by the deadline.
