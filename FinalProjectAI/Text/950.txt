Penn Dental Medicine 3-4 BioDental Program



Students admitted to this program spend three years at Muhlenberg and four years at the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, graduating with a B.S. degree in Biology from Muhlenberg (after completing year one of dental school) and a D.D.S. degree from the University of Pennsylvania.




Learn More



















SUNY College of Optometry 3-4 Combined Program



An agreement between Muhlenberg College and SUNY College of Optometry allows selected students to enter SUNY College of Optometry at the end of their third year of study at Muhlenberg.




Learn More



















Temple School of Medicine Early Assurance Program



Temple University School of Medicine, St. Luke's Hospital and Health Network and Muhlenberg College are partners in an integrated undergraduate/affiliated hospital/medical school program for Early Assurance Applicants.




Learn More



















Boston University School of Medicine 



The partnership with Boston University School of Medicine provides Muhlenberg students early access to one of the nation’s premiere medical schools and gives qualified students a clear path to acceptance midway through their Muhlenberg career.




Learn More
