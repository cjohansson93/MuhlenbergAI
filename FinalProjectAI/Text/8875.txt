All new incoming students (first-year and transfer) must complete the following requirements:
Step 1:  In preparation for your arrival to campus, please click on the links below to read the following information.

Welcome Letter
Notice of Privacy Practices 
Information about Meningococcal Disease and Vaccination 
Prematriculation Immunization Policy 
Policy for Exemption from Immunization

Step 2:  Print the following and bring to your health care provider to complete.

Physical Exam Record

If your last physical exam was more than 12 months before the first day of class, call your health care provider office to schedule a physical exam

Immunization Record

Step 3:  Log into PyraMed Health Portal
Complete the following forms on the PyraMed Health Portal (Click on âMy Formsâ tab)

Immunization History

You will be prompted to upload your Immunization Record within the form


Medical History

Health Insurance

Consent for Treatment, Notice of Privacy Practices, & Communication Preferences
Emergency Contacts

Health Forms must be submitted through the PyraMed Health Portal.  Please call Health Services (484-664-3199) with any concerns.
To access the PyraMed Health Portal:

First, access the Muhlenberg College Application Portal (login.muhlenberg.edu)
Enter your Muhlenberg user name and password (supplied by Informational Technology)
Click on the icon for PyraMed Health Portal

Refer to the âWhat You Need to Know about Technology @ Muhlenberg Collegeâ letter you received from Information Technology to access the Application Portal. 

Please be certain that all health form information is completed by July 1 (for fall entry) or Jan 5 (for spring entry). You will be notified by "Messages" on the PyraMed Health Portal regarding any incomplete information. If you cannot complete your forms by the deadline, notify Health Services.
