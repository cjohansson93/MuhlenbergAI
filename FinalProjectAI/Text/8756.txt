Entertainment/Restaurants

Lots of popular restaurants in our area offer student discounts. Please check out this website with more info!

Through the iHeart Muhlenberg Program, you can receive discounts at many businesses in the Lehigh Valley by showing your College ID.
https://www.moneylogue.com/save-on-food-your-student-discount/


 

Student Days and Discounts at Local Movie Theatres

Movie Tavern- on Hamilton Blvd in Allentown, PA. Every Thursday, students of all ages & school faculty can enjoy blockbuster movies* for only $6 and a FREE 46oz popcorn. Simply show your school ID.
AMC Theatres- Allentown, PA. Get tickets for just $5 every Tuesday by signing up to be an AMC Stubs Insider Member, it's completely FREE!  
New Vision Theatres- Allentown, PA. $5.00 Ticket Tuesdays and they offer a student discount for any other day that you come in to see a movie just by showing your student ID.
Civic Theatre-Allentown, PA. Student tickets are $4 on Tuesdays and $6 every other day.
