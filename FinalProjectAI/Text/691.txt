Connie Wolfe, Ph.D.
 
Associate ProfessorSocial Psychology Moyer Hall - Room 215conniewolfe@muhlenberg.edu484.664.3686 Fax: 484.664.5627 Professor Wolfe's Homepage
 
Background B.A., Ohio University; M.A., University at Buffalo; Ph.D., University of Michigan
Professor Wolfe teaches courses about social psychology, research methods, advanced research, contemporary racism, and intergroup dialogue. Her research focuses on several topics including ideologies that sustain prejudice and microaggressions on campus. Visit Dr. Wolfe's website for more information about her classes, research, office hours, and contact information.
In May 2017, Dr. Wolfe was awarded the Paul C. Empie Memorial Award for Teaching and Service at Muhlenberg's 169th Commencement.
