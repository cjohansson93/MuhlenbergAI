IFC
 
The Interfraternity Council (IFC) is the governing body for the three North American Interfraternity Conference (NIC) organizations represented at Muhlenberg College; Alpha Tau Omega, Delta Tau Delta, and Zeta Beta Tau. 
 
IFC Executive Board:
 
 
Fraternities: 
Alpha Epsilon Pi, Alpha Beta Chapter
Founded at Muhlenberg College in 1988. Recolonized in 2019.
Learn more about Alpha Epsilon Pi.
 
Alpha Tau Omega, Alpha Iota Chapter
Founded at Muhlenberg College in 1881. Rechartered in 2011.
Learn more about Alpha Tau Omega.
 
Delta Tau Delta, Theta Chi Chapter
Founded at Muhlenberg College in 1998. Rechartered in 2004.
Learn more about Delta Tau Delta.
 
Phi Kappa Tau, Eta Chapter
Founded at Muhlenberg College in 1918. Recolonizing in 2020.
President - TBD Spring 2020
Learn more about Phi Kappa Tau.
 
Zeta Beta Tau, Alpha Nu Chapter
Founded at Muhlenberg College in 1970. Rechartered in 2015.
Learn more about Zeta Beta Tau.
