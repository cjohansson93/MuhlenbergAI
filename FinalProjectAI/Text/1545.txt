Catholic Campus Ministry (CCM)


 
 
Catholic Campus Ministry, a ministry of the Catholic Diocese of Allentown, seeks to assist students of Muhlenberg and Cedar Crest Colleges to deepen their faith in Jesus Christ.  CCM provides an environment in which students, of the Catholic Faith, are invited to share and express their faith, by engaging with other members in fellowship and community.
All Roman Catholic Students currently enrolled at Muhlenberg are members of CCM.
