The Muhlenberg College School of Graduate Studies offers graduate degrees and certificates with multiple pathways that provide flexibility for the adult learner to create their graduate experience. Programs are delivered in a format that blends in-person classroom discussions with online and virtual components.
Learn more about Applied Analytics.
Learn more about Organizational Leadership.













Graduate Studies
Graduate Degrees and Certificates
Our programs are designed for the adult learner who aims to develop their professional career and identities. 










Master's in Organizational Leadership



A master's degree program designed to prepare professionals to guide organizations in strategy, management, planning, and continuous improvement.




Learn More



















Master's in Applied Analytics



A master's degree program designed to teach skilled professionals to apply analytical methods for informed organizational decision-making.




Learn More



















Certificate in Organizational Leadership



Prepares professionals to succeed in current or future leadership positions, responsibilities, and opportunities. 




Learn More



















Certificate in Applied Analytics



Gain specialized skills in data analytics to fill organizational roles that analyze data and find more efficient ways to operate.
