Instructions for Student Researchers
 




General Instructions 


Required reading for all Muhlenberg student researchers.



 
 



Ethics Proposal


Before conducting research at Muhlenberg, you must have ethics approval. Download the proposal application and, when completed, submit it to the Psychology Department Coordinator of Human Participants Research for expedited review.



 
 



Detailed Instructions 


Will your participants be completing your study in person at an assigned location, or will they complete the study online in a location of their choosing? Please choose the appropriate set of instructions:
