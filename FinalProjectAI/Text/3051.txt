librick named golf coach,equipment manager


Muhlenberg has hired John Librick as director of golf and head equipment room manger. In his role as director of golf, he will be 

head coach of the men’s team and have administrative duties for the women’s program.

Librick comes to Muhlenberg from nearby Center Valley Club, where since 2000 he had served as outside operations manager and assistant professional. In addition to representing the golf course at expos locally and nationally, he provided one-on-one golf instruction to men and women of all ages (8 to 80) and coached golfers in a “clinic” setting in groups ranging in size from two to approximately 20 individuals.

Librick annually handled around 30 external tournaments, which fielded up to 300 golfers. He also presented several programs on the rules and etiquette of golf to the Executive Women’s Golf Association; conducted a presentation at Lehigh University on the dynamics of how golf can be utilized to create business networks and opportunities; and volunteered golf instruction services for Big Brothers and Big Sisters of the Lehigh Valley.

A 1985 graduate of Trenton State College, Librick also has considerable management experience in the corporate world, with eight years as an associate manager at Prudential Insurance Company and five as a call center supervisor at Binney & Smith. 

Librick inherits a Mule men’s program that has won four Centennial Conference championships since 1997.
