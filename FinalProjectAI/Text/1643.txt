 Muhlenberg College To Participate In Lee National Denim Day Day Long Event To Promote Breast Cancer Awareness 



On Friday, October 7, Muhlenberg students, faculty and staff will show their support for breast cancer awareness by wearing their finest pair of jeans on Lee National Denim Day. 


	 Monday, October 3, 2005  01:59 PM

In addition to this positive show of support, the day will also comprise of a fundraising campaign in Seegers Union, where all proceeds will benefit the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation, and two panel presentations by health professionals Jyoti Gopal, M.D., and Amy Kern, ‘99, P.A.-C, from Muhlenberg Primary Care in Bethlehem. These will also be in the Seegers Union in room 108-109 at 11:30 a.m. and 12:30 p.m., respectively.
