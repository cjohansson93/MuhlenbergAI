Refrigerators
There are two different sizes of refrigerators that are suitable for Residence Halls. Refrigerators must be 4.1 amps or less.
 
Small Size

The small cube size range from 1.3 -1.8 cu.ft.
 
Large Size

The large cube size range from 4.3 - 4.9 cu. ft. No larger than 4.9 cu.ft. permitted

 Microwaves
Microwaves may pull no more than 600-700 watts and there can only be one per room.
