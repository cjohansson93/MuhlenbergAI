Talent Scholarships



All students are eligible for Talent Scholarship consideration.
We offer Talent Scholarships in Art, Dance, Film Studies, Music, and Theater.  The scholarships range from $1,000-$4,000 a year.
You may apply for more than one discipline.
To schedule an audition or portfolio review, please contact the following department heads.
