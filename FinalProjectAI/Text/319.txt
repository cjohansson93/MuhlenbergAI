Moving Services
As part of the Muhlenberg community, you may be able to access adjusted rates on moving services through E&I Cooperative Services, a purchasing cooperative serving the needs of those in the education field. If you have worked with a specific company in the past, check with the E&I representative to see if those services are available.

UniGroup: provides moving, transportation and relocation services, both domestically and globally. UniGroup includes many companies, such as Mayflower, United Van Lines and Armstrong, under their umbrella.

Contact: Tim Grimes, 800-845-6327
EICoop@unitedvanlines.com
EICoop@Mayflower.com

SIRVA Worldwide Relocation & Moving: provides end-to-end moving and relocation services, both domestically and globally. SIRVA includes many companies, such as Allied Van Lines and northAmerican, under their umbrella.

Contact: Bobbi Maniglia, 440-476-0970
maniglia@SIRVA.com
northamerican.com
