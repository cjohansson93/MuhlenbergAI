The Underground at Martin Luther
The Underground is a multi-purpose facility located in the basement of Martin Luther which features:

Stage Area
Area for group or private study
Full Kitchen
Large Screen TV 

The Underground is available for informal use but also can be scheduled for use by student groups and is often used for theater performances, coffeehouses, open mic nights, and dance parties. If you would like to schedule the Underground, please call the Office of Residential Services at 484-664-3180.
