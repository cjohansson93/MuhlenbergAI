Muhlenberg College/University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine 3-4 BioDental Program

Overview Students admitted to this program spend three years at Muhlenberg and four years at the School of Dental Medicine, graduating with a B.S. degree in Biology from Muhlenberg (after completing year one of dental school) and a D.D.S. degree from the University of Pennsylvania. Transfer students are not eligible for this program.
Students admitted to the program are guaranteed admission to the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine upon:

Meeting the Muhlenberg degree requirements
Attaining the minimum required GPA of 3.5 CUM/3.5 Science
Filing a formal dental school application to the Associated American Dental Schools Application Service (AADSAS) prior to September 1 of the student's junior year
Attaining at minimum of 20 in all subject areas of the Dental Admission Test (DAT)

Academic Requirements 
To be considered for the program, students must:

Attain a score on the combined Critical Reading/Math SAT; combined Evidence-Based Reading and Writing/Math SAT of 1360 (SAT-R); ACT Composite score of 32 or greater
Rank in the top 5-10% of their high school class
Have taken a strong schedule of AP and Honor classes during their high school career
Have specific, hands-on DENTAL experience (through internships, jobs, research, shadowing dentist, etc.)

Deadlines

Personal interview with Muhlenberg College Admissions before February 1.
Completed first-year application to Muhlenberg College Office of Admission before February 1 of senior year.
Completed Penn Dental Supplement to Muhlenberg (admissions@muhlenberg.edu or Office of Admissions, 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, PA 18104) by February 1.

(Note: Candidates do not interview at Penn for this program; however, the interview at Muhlenberg is mandatory.)
Typical Notification Schedule 

Finalists selected by Muhlenberg committee and sent to Penn Dental for review: February


Muhlenberg notifies students that they are finalists: March
Students notified of acceptance into the program: Early April
