Ten Muhlenberg athletes recently were named to the spring Centennial Conference Sportsmanship Team.

Each member institution was permitted to nominate one student from each spring program who exemplifies the best in sportsmanship. 
Nominations were submitted by the athletic departments and head coaches.

The Mules honored and their sports are:

• Taylor Armstrong, senior, women’s lacrosse
• Garrett Carpenter, senior, men’s golf
• Adam Carter, senior, men’s track and field
• Hayley Cush, junior, women’s golf
• Alyssa Epstein, freshman, softball
• Jamie Goldman, senior, men’s tennis
• Eric Hammond, junior, baseball
• Erika Koop, senior, women’s track and field
• Andrew Lolley, junior, men’s lacrosse
• Carly Rotfeld, junior, women’s tennis
