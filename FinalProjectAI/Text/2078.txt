Biology Department Facilities
Muhlenberg College Greenhouse





The Muhlenberg College Greenhouse is a climate controlled facility containing live plant collections and is located on the third floor of the Shankweiler Building. The greenhouse contains a diverse teaching collection and is the site of a number of faculty and student research projects. Students are welcome to visit the greenhouse to develop projects, and re-pot and cultivate plants.
GREENHOUSE TOUR
