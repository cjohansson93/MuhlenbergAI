Mathematics Courses Waiting List Policy
As of Fall Registration 2021, all course waiting lists are now managed internally in WorkDay. If a course has reached capacity, you will be prompted to add your name to the waiting list for that course.If you have additional questions, please reach out to the course instructor/faculty or email the department Administrator, Traci Hilbert (tracihilbert@muhlenberg.edu), and she will help with any questions you may have.
