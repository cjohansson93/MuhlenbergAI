Functions
The committee has responsibility for reviewing proposals and disbursing funds for two categories: Lectures, which supports a single, small-scale, faculty initiated event related to the academic program and Forum, a series of programs, thematically linked, that will distinguish the College regionally or nationally. The committee is also responsible for requiring a post-event report including information such as attendance.(December 2010)
