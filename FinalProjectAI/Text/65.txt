Members of the Spanish Club at Muhlenberg College engage in a broad spectrum of extracurricular activities which relate to the study of culture, history, politics, and society. If you're curious about the Spanish Club, its cultural heritage, or current developments, you may want to consider joining our club. By becoming a member you will enjoy the opportunity to visit art exhibitions, attend concerts and shows, view and discuss films and TV shows with your peers and faculty, meet knowledgeable guest speakers, taste foods, and much more.
