Campus Health and Well-being



A message from President Harring to the Muhlenberg Community

Friday, October 15, 2021

To the Muhlenberg Community:
On Friday, October 22, in lieu of classes, the College will hold Muhlenberg R & R (Restore & Reconnect), a day devoted to learning activities, reflection spaces, and support resources aimed at prioritizing the mental health and emotional well-being of the campus community.
Please take a look at the video below to hear about the activities and programming that will be available. Specific details and a complete schedule will be shared next week. If you have a suggestion for programming that can be incorporated into the day please email deanofstudents@muhlenberg.edu.

Take care and be well,
Kathleen HarringPresident
