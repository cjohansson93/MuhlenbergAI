Services
The Copy Center operates many types of machines to support the campus community. Our services include:
- Black and White Copying
- Color Copying
- Black and White or Color output from electronic files
- Scan to PDF
- Wide Format (Posters, Banners)
- Envelope printing including Data Merge
- Folding
-Cutting
-Padding
- GBC binding
