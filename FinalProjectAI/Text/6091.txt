Wrestlers Win 3 of 4 at Gettysburg
 Wednesday, February 8, 2012 - page 2







					 



The Muhlenberg wrestling team continued to perform well against Centennial Conference competition, winning three of four contested bouts at Gettysburg.

The four Mules have combined to go 16-12 in their CC dual matches.

Senior 184-pounder John Sternlicht improved his CC record to a team-best 6-1 by pinning his opponent in just 29 seconds. Sternlicht (right) has eight
wins this season, five by pin.

Senior Kevin Homan ran his record at 149 to 26-7 with his sixth pin of the season. Junior 141-pounder Matt Parker notched his first
career CC dual-match win, earning a 2-1 decision.

The Bullets picked up five wins by forfeit and won the match, 35-15.
