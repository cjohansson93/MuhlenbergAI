Mayden Runs at National Cross Country Meet
 Sunday, November 20, 2011







					 


  



Junior Samantha Mayden of the Muhlenberg women’s cross country team finished 195th at the NCAA Championships, hosted by Wis.-Oshkosh at Lake
Breeze Golf Course in Winneconne, Wis.

Mayden’s finish was the second-best ever by a Mule runner at the national meet. Karen Mount was 127th in 2005. Mayden actually finished ahead of more runners,
as she was 195th out of 277 and Mount was 127th out of 205.
