Muhlenberg College Faculty And The Civic Theatre Join To Present Film Series 



The Muhlenberg College faculty and the Civic Theatre will join forces to present the Muhlenberg Film Forum. Created as part of the Civic Theatre's 19th Street Film series.


	 Tuesday, February 8, 2005  10:04 AM

The Muhlenberg College faculty and the Civic Theatre will join forces to present the Muhlenberg Film Forum. Created as part of the Civic Theatre's 19th Street Film series, this series will consist of post-film discussions, led by Muhlenberg College faculty on four consecutive Wednesday nights.  The forum schedule is as follows: February 16 — Bad Education; discussion led by Erika Sutherland, Department of Languages, Literatures and Cultures.February 23 — A Very Long Engagement; discussion led by Eileen Ketchum, Department of Langauges, Literatures and Cultures.March 2 —Hotel Rwanda; discussion led by Chris Herrick, Department of Political Science March 9 — Merchant of Venice; discussion lead by Tom Cartelli, Department of English.All films start at 7:30 p.m., and discussions will follow. The 19th Street Theatre Film Series runs throughout February and March.  The entire schedule is available at www.civictheatre.com/film/schedule.html. The forums are part of the ticket price for that evening. For ticket information, call the Civic Theatre box office at 610-432-0888. ReleasedFebruary 8, 2005 For more information, contactJill Lowery 484-664-3235
