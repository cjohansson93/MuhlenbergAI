Victoria Marsala '17
Victoria Marsala '17 is a psychology and media & communications double major. She has a strong interest in public relations and hopes to land a career in the field upon graduation, hopefully tying in her passion for either music or fashion.
In addition to being a fall 2016 intern for the public relations department, Victoria is a campus tour guide and a supervisor for Muhlenberg’s Institute of Public Opinion.
