About the Committee


Members of the Employee Wellness Committee are expected to:

Actively support the mission of the committee to provide greater health awareness by cultivating an environment designed to support healthy lifestyle choices for both our employees and their families.
Serve a three-year term as a voting committee member
Attend monthly committee meetings (first Tuesday of each month)
Serve an active role in committee communications 
Actively promote and plan Employee Wellness Committee events and initiatives
Participate in Employee Wellness Committee events, as schedule permits. 
Serve as an advocate for wellness and wellness programming  in the department or office of employment
Complete other duties as assigned
