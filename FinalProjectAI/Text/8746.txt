In applying for an academic partnership, you may apply Early Decision or Regular Decision. However, if you apply Early Decision, you are indicating your intention to enroll at Muhlenberg whether you are approved for the academic partnership or not (i.e. you might be admitted to Muhlenberg, but not to the University of Pennsylvania 3-4 Dental Program).

For the following programs, you must apply to Muhlenberg before January 1 of your senior year in high school.

Muhlenberg College/SUNY College of Optometry



For the following programs, you must apply to Muhlenberg before February 1 of your senior year in high school.

Muhlenberg College/University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine 3-4 BioDental Program




A personal interview with Muhlenberg College Admissions is required for these programs and must be completed before their stated deadline.


Please note that each program has specific requirements and supplemental application materials. Be sure to read the program-specific pages carefully.

For the following programs, your Muhlenberg application and program application should be completed simultaneously:

Muhlenberg College/SUNY College of Optometry

For the following programs, students will submit their applications after they have enrolled at Muhlenberg:

Villanova University Charles Widger School of Law 3-3 Program
Muhlenberg College/Temple University School of Medicine Early Assurance Program
Engineering Dual-Degree Program with Columbia University
American University Kogod School of Business
Boston University School of Public Health
Boston University School of Medicine
Boston University Questrom School of Business
Lehigh University Master of Science in Management
Penn State College of Medicine
Moravian College Music Certification (K-12)
Lutheran Theological Seminary of Philadelphia
