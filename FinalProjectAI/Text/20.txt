Spanish Major Requirements
The Spanish Major consists of 9 courses total beyond the SPN 204 course. To be accepted as a major in Spanish, a student must have an average of at least 2.0 in the intermediate language courses (203-204) taken at Muhlenberg College or demonstrate a fluent command of the language.
Two Advanced Language and Communication Courses: Students may take either SPN-301 or SPN-303. If SPN 301 or SPN-303 is fulfilled through placement, the student substitutes an extra 400-level class to complete the 9-course major.
