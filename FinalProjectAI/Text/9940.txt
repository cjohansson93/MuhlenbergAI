Andrew Lutz-Long 
Adjunct Professor, Music Baker Center for the Arts
Department of MusicMuhlenberg College2400 Chew StreetAllentown, PA 18104
Andrew Lutz-Long is College Organist at Muhlenberg, where he helps to plan and lead weekly Chapel worship, any festival services, and assisting the music department as needed. He is also an adjunct professor of music, teaching organ lessons. On a full-time basis, Drew serves as Director of Music Ministries at Trinity Lutheran Church in Perkasie. He directs the choirs, oversees the Concert Series, and assists the worship and educational life of Trinity in various ways - roles which he has filled since graduating from Lebanon Valley College in 2007. Drew also serves as a liaison for the Property Team, fulfilling his interest in architecture and historical buildings. In addition to his work at Muhlenberg and Trinity, Drew is the Artistic Director of the Valley Choral Society which regularly performs in Bucks and Montgomery counties.
