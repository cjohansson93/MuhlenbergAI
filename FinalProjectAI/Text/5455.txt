Men’s Golf Eighth at F&M


The Muhlenberg men’s golf team finished eighth at the Bent Creek Invitational. Host Franklin & Marshall ran away with the tournament, posting an extraordinary
score of 297, 19 strokes ahead of the second-place teams. The Mules were in a pack of nine teams that were separated by just 13 strokes. Junior Biff Slocum shot
a team-best 80.
