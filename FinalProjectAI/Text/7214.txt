Wrestling Open Up at King's
 Sunday, November 3, 2013







					 





The Muhlenberg wrestling team sent six competitors to the season-opening Monarch Invitational at King’s yesterday.

Of the six, sophomore Che Chengsupanimit (right) was the only one to return home with a win. The 149-pounder scored a 7-3 decision
against an opponent from Lycoming in the consolation bracket.
