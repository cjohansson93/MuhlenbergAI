Majors have immediate access, even as first years to up-to-date and top-of-the-line production equipment offering a wide variety of high-end production equipment including  professional digital video cameras, digital audio recorders, a variety of directional and omnidirectional microphones, lighting kits and tripod systems, and various production accessories.   















Audio



The department offers Custom-built Audio Kits, Lavalier Mics, Mic Sticks, Rode Mics, Shotgun Mics, Wireless Mics, Tascams, Voice Recorders, Boompoles, Windscreens, 4 channel field recorder, and PodCast Kits.















Cameras



The department offers DSLR-5D series, DSLR-T series, DJI Osmo Pocket, 360 camera, Blackmagic 6k, and a Go-Pro. 















Lighting



The department offers LED Light Kits, ARRI Light Kits, On-Camera LED kits, Gel Kits, Soft Boxes, Flag Kits, C Stands and Flags, Dimmers, LED Fresnel Kits, RGB Panel Lights and Bounces. 















Editing Software



The department contains two classrooms with iMac's and portable iMac's, and students in production courses get access to an array of multimedia editing software including Adobe Creative Cloud















Stability & Movement



The Department offers Tripods, Monopods, Sliders, Steady Cams, Cages, Dolly and Track, Phone Tripods, Mini Manfrotto, and a Ronin Motorized Gimbal.  















Video Game Lab



The department offers a portable video game lab with an array of gaming systems and games for rental.
