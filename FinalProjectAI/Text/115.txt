Dr. Martin Lo Sasso '15: Prehealth Spotlight



Surgical resident Dr. Martin Lo Sasso '15 used his Muhlenberg experiences in the classroom and through study abroad as a springboard for his medical career. 


By:



  Jonathan Schlegel  



	 Wednesday, April 21, 2021  11:26 AM


In his current position at St. Luke's Health Network in Bethlehem, PA, he reflects on how the opportunities and relationships he had at Muhlenberg led to where he is today. The chemistry major was a prehealth student at Muhlenberg and took advantage of high-impact practices, like a full year of study abroad in Scotland.
