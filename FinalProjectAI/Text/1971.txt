Shuttle Service

The Muhlenberg Grey ShuttleThe Grey Shuttle for Fall 2021 provided by Palmeri Transportation.Get details, including the shuttle schedule
Be advised that changes to the College's shuttle services will be coming in June 2022.
The Break Shuttle Runs on the last day of class and the day before classes resume. The Break Shuttle goes to the local bus stations and the ABE Airport. Details for this shuttle are mass mailed to the students up to three weeks prior to the scheduled Break.
Airport Shuttle New airport shuttle service for our students who have a need to travel using the Philadelphia and Newark Airports. Shuttle details and online reservation form
Personal TravelA1 Limousine is available for your personal travel needs and you will receive The Muhlenberg discount. 
Van ReservationVan reservation is available to staff and faculty.  Login and access our online form
