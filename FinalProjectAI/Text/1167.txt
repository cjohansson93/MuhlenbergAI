Learn About What We Do
Explore emerging technologies and facilitate the effective use of technology in support of language learning, research, and service learning. Assist students daily in the use of language learning, and also support faculty in the following areas:
• Instruction within the LC Commons and in the classroom
• Management of course materials in Blackboard
• Assistance with departmental events
• Online FL placement exams
• Design, development, and maintenance of departmental website
