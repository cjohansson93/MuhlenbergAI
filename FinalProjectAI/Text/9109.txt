Muhlenberg College offers a rich experience to all of our students. Every day at Muhlenberg brings new opportunities for intellectual growth and fulfillment, not only within the classroom but throughout our campus. In any given 24 hours, a student might see a world premiere show, attend a talk by a national leader, volunteer in the local area and much more. Our community is lively, caring and diverse. See what is here for you to discover. Below and in this section are a few of the many ways Muhlenberg College supports our students.
Muhlenberg College School of Graduate Studies creates a personal experience for each of our students starting with an assigned graduate advisor who will stay with you through your program of studies. We will work with you before you enroll to determine if we are the right solution for you and your goals. From application to new graduate student orientation: We will help you complete the application process and answer any questions you have about our programs; we will help you navigate enrollment, financial aid, and any other college service. After orientation and the start of classes, we will be your success coach, mentor, and resource assuring you get the most out of your time with us. All of our graduate advisors are doctorally prepared with extensive workforce and career advisement experience. Our goal is to be proactive and help you reach your academic and career goals.
