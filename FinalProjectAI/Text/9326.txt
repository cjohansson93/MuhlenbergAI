'Berg Hosts Health Care Panel



Muhlenberg College will host âHealth Care Insurance Reform:  Now What?â an interdisciplinary panel discussion with âBerg faculty and administrators on Monday, April 26 at 4 p.m. in Seegers Union, room 108-109.  This event is free and open to the public. 


	 Friday, April 23, 2010  11:45 AM

Congress has passed health insurance reform legislation, and the impact will be felt in upcoming years.  Muhlenberg experts on management, business, public health, history, politics and economics will offer their views and discuss possible health care futures with the audience.
Topics raised will include:

The legislation in the long-term narrative of public health in America (Dan Wilson, History)
Economic impacts of the legislation (Sam Laposata, Economics)
The effects on businesses of different types and sizes (Roland Kushner, Business)
Possible effects on the College as an employer (Jason Feiertag, Assistant Treasurer)
The political impact (Chris Borick, Political Science)
