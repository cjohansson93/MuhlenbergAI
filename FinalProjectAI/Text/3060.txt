Player’s Players recognized



The Muhlenberg student-athletes pictured below won the “Player’s
Player” Awards for their respective teams in 2008-09.

The Player’s Player is each team’s most valuable player, as
decided upon by a team vote. In addition to athletic performance, the
spirit of the award encompasses qualities of leadership, loyalty and
support of teammates.
