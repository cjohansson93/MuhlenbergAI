Volleyball Sweeps FDU for 10th Win
 Thursday, October 4, 2012







					 



The Muhlenberg volleyball team recorded its second 3-0 win in as many nights, sweeping FDU-Florham on the road. Set scores were 25-17, 25-22 and 25-18.

The victory gave the Mules 10 for the season, matching their total from last year.

Three Muhlenberg players had double figures in digs, led by freshman Chelsea Kaser with 17. Sophomore Alyssa Schell (left) added a
season-high 13, and freshman Vanessa Pulice contributed 12. Pulice also had team highs in assists (24) and aces (4).

Freshman Katherine Boll led the attack with eight kills.
