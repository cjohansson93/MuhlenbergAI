Women's Golf 3rd at McDaniel; Women's Tennis Opens at ITA
 Sunday, September 22, 2013 - page 2







					 






The Muhlenberg women’s golf team finished third out of four complete teams at yesterday’s McDaniel Fall Invitational.

The Mules had a team score of 400, a nine-stroke improvement over their total at the season-opening Dickinson Invitational the previous weekend.

Junior Christina Bryde came in eighth, just two strokes away from third, with a 91. Freshman Emily Unrue carded a 95.

Junior Laura Glogovsky (right) was Muhlenberg’s third finisher with a score of 106, two strokes off her career best.

The women’s tennis team opened its fall season at the ITA Southeast Regional, hosted by Mary Washington.

Three Mules won a match at the tournament. Freshman Jamie Rosenblum and junior Ashlee Ludwig (left) opened with wins
in the Blue 2 and Blue 5 singles draws, respectively, while sophomore Jenny Levenson was victorious in a Blue 6 singles consolation match.
