Fees
Office visit:  No charge
Over-the counter medications: Fees range from $0 - $5
Prescription medications:  Fees range from $5 - $30 (but most are $15 or less)
Crutches:  $30
Allergy Shots:  $2 per injection
Emergency Contraceptive Pills $25
PPD Skin Test $10
Rapid Strep Test:  $6
Rapid Flu Test:  $20
Payment (cash, check, credit card, Berg Bucks) is expected at the time of service. Students experiencing hardship with payment should contact the Director of Health Services.
Students and parents should be aware that the student is responsible for any costs associated with care and services received on and off campus.
