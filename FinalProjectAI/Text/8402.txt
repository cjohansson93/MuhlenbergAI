Muhlenberg College Employee Reporting Resource Guide
Urgent Concern or Emergency
If you or someone you know feels threatened, if you feel your or someone else's safety is at risk, or if you or someone you know is in need of immediate medical attention:
Call Campus Safety & Police at (484) 664-3110 / Ext. 3110 
Or Call 911
Internal Resources
In the absense of an urgent concern/emergency, Muhlenberg College offers a variety of ways to report discrimination, harassment, sexual misconduct, relationship violence, stalking or retaliatory harassment related to or involving students, faculty, and/or staff:

Online Incident Reporting
In Person

Jill Walsh, VP for Human Resources, Haas Bldg., 3rd Floor

Email:

Christine Wechsler, Interim Director of Equity and Title IX

Please note that the College's ability to respond to a situation depends on the amount of detail provided in a report. In addition, the resources listed above are not strictly confidential, however, information is kept as private as possible and with careful consideration to the person(s) impacted.
