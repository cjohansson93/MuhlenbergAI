Directions 
Located in Allentown, Pa., in the vibrant Lehigh Valley, Muhlenberg is less than 90 miles west of New York City and 50 miles north of Philadelphia. 
By Car:If using a GPS, please enter this address: 
2400 West Chew St.Allentown, PA 18104
Follow Interstate Route 78 (east-west) or I-476 (Northeast Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike) to U.S. Route 22, then U.S. Route 22 or I-78 to Allentown exits. From 22, take Cedar Crest Boulevard exit south (left) to third traffic light, take a left on Chew Street, and follow Chew Street about one mile to the campus. From 78, take the Cedar Crest Boulevard exit north (right) and go six traffic lights. Take a right on to Chew Street and follow W. Chew Street about one mile to the campus.
By Air:Lehigh Valley International Airport is 15 minutes by car from the Muhlenberg campus. The airport is served by American, Continental, Delta, Northwest, United and US Airways, as well as their âexpressâ extensions. For additional information contact the Lehigh Valley International Airport at 800-FLY-LVIA (800-359-5842). Alternatively, fly into Philadelphia International Airport, and the College is 75 minutes by car almost directly north.
By Bus:Greyhound-Trailways and Trans-Bridge Lines have depot facilities in Allentown. Bus service runs from JFK, LaGuardia and Newark airports to Allentown. Newark is closest.
Allentown Bus Terminal325 W Hamilton StAllentown, PA 18101610-434-6188
