Assistant Professor, Media & Communicationkateranieri@muhlenberg.edu MuhlenbergMemoriesProject.comWalson 103
Background
B.A., Cedar Crest CollegeM.A., Ed.D., Northern Illinois University
Kathryn L. Ranieri's current work is a collaborative partnership with an archivist and a digital cultures technologist to engage documentary research students in creating digital stories based on institutional history as part of the Muhlenberg Memories Project. As part of community outreach with these collaborative partners, Dr. Ranieri works with students and The Allentown Band to preserve, digitize and make accessible historical materials as well as capture oral histories of band members.
