Go behind the scenes with our Instagram Takeovers!
Students and alumni take the reins of our Insta for the day to show you a little bit of what it's like to be a Berg Theatre & Dance student. Check out our Instagram page too!





Memento: Mori: Triptych
For the last production of the Mnemonic Theatre Festival, join Hannah Michelson '22 as she shares information about the immersive, innovative experience Memento: Mori: Triptych. 



 

En Route: A Site-Responsive Dance Festival
Tune in for our first-ever faculty takeover with dance professor Megan Flynn! Megan shows you behind the scenes of En Route on opening night.



 

Unstable Connections
Tune in as Emily Kitchin '21 takes you behind the scenes of all three episodes of the hilarious webseries, Unstable Connections. Experience another production in the Mnemonic Theatre Festival as well as life on zoom in three parts. 



 

10x10 (or, The Decameron)
Join cast member 10x10 (or, The Decameron), Ruhani Singh '23 as she takes you behind the scenes of this innovative theatrical event, part of the Mnemonic Theatre Festival. 



 

Ephemerality: Dances In Time
To celebrate the second in-person dance concert since February '20, Kate McCowan '22 takes you backstage of the dance program's new and innovative production, Ephemerality: Dances In Time. 



 

A List
Join Kalia Rucker '22 as they take you behind the scenes of Gertude Stein's play, A List. This short, endless play marks the fourth production in the Mnemonic Theatre Festival. 



 

Run Glue Tree Pour Dive: A Re-Membering
Meet Bekka Broyles '22 as she shares her insight into the innovative and experimental prouduction, Run Glue Tree Pour Dive; another creation for the Mnemonic Theatre Festival.



 

Far Away
Learn more about the second production of the Mnemonic Theatre Festival, Far Away as Philippa Edstrand-Hearn takes you behind the virtual stage and opens the show with you!



 

Senior Dance Showcase
Tune in as Emi Giuseffi '21 takes you behind the scenes of this year's Senior Dance Showcase. This performance marked the dance program's first live performance in over a year! 



 

MAGIC
Join the Mercy Olajobi '21 as she takes you behind the scenes of the world premiere virtual play, MAGIC! This play also marks the first prodduction of this season's Mnemonic Theatre Festival.   





Muhlenberg Dance Association's DanceWorks Series 
Join the two student coordinators of this production, Lilianne Gering '21 and Dani Barrett '22, as they take you behind the scenes of this fall's DanceWorks series!  



 

Dance Practices 1
Meet the current dancers in Dance Practices 1 as they share their own unique style! In this course, the dancers have been discussing dance-on-camera and the relationship between social media and dance. 



 

MTA's Fish Project Play Festival 
Tune in as the two student coordinators of this production, Ali Rohrbaugh '22 and Jaxson Goldsmith '23, take you behind the scenes of the innovative Fish Project Play Festival! 



 

Sedehi Diversity Project: Ensemble Members 
Join ensemble members of this year's Sedehi Diversity Project to learn more about this powerful documentary theatre piece!
