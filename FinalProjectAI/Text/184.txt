Wetland Ecology

Students research wetland plants and present their information to the class.   They learn the ecological services provided by wetlands and the factors that determine wetland boundaries.  Wetland conservation is also covered. 
Grades: 8-12
Time Needed: 3 hours + research time before the field study

Muhlenberg College Wetland Ecology Module/Unit (PDF File - 4.4 MB)
