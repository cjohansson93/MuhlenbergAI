carnevale namedathlete of the week


Muhlenberg freshman Stephanie Carnevale was named Centennial Conference women’s indoor track and field athlete of the week.

Carnevale placed in the top three in four events at the Muhlenberg Invitational. She won the 55-meter hurdles, and she also was second in the long and triple jumps and third in the 55 meters.

Carnevale’s time of 8.97 in the 55 hurdles was a significant improvement from the 9.22 she ran in the opening meet of the season two weeks prior and
put her in fifth place on the Mules’ all-time performance list. She also landed in ninth on the performance list in the 55 meters with a time of 7.87 seconds in her first
time running the event for Muhlenberg.
