Erika Iyengar Awarded Donald B. Hoffman Research Fellowship



The award was presented at the College's 2021 Honors Convocation.


	 Friday, May 14, 2021  08:15 AM

Named in honor of Dr. Donald B. Hoffman â32, this fellowship supports faculty in scholarship, research or creative activity which, results in publication, performance or exhibition while enhancing the reputation of the College. This year's recipient was Erika Iyengar, professor of biology.
