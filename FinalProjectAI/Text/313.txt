Online work request
To submit an online work request, please folllow these instructions:

Select Submit a Work Order below
Log into OneLogin 
Enter the Location (building name)
Enter the room # and / or name of space
Work Requested Field: provide as much information as you can to describe work needed and any other information that would be helpful in resolving the concerns

Please Note:

Photos can be attached to the request form
Emails will be sent out as the work order is processed

 General Information

Please allow 2-3 business days for a non-emergency request.
For an emergency request (ie, Lost power, burning smell, water overflowing) Please  contact Plant Operations between the hours of 8:30 am- 5:00 pm at, 484-664-3400. After hours, contact Campus safety at 484-664-3110.
*Request for lock changes and window air condition units must be requested through, and submitted, only by the Office of Residential Services, 484-664-3180.
You must use a valid Muhlenberg College e-mail address.
You must submit a phone number
