Student LifeHealth ServicesClinical Services 











Clinical services, which are provided by registered nurses, nurse practitioner, and physicians include:


Injury, Illness, and First Aid care

Concussion screens, blisters, corneal abrasions, bug bites, minor burns, ear flushes, eye exams, eye flushes, hot packs, suture and staple removal, splinter removal, sprains, strain, wound care, earaches, infections, allergies, cough, cold, flu, sore throat, sinus infections, urinary tract infections, abdominal pain


Wellness visits

Flu shots, tuberculosis testing, allergy injections, birth control, gynecological services, free STI testing, emergency contraception pills


Skin conditions

Fungal infections, cold sores, canker sores, impetigo, lice, rashes, poison, ringworm, scabies, shingles, wound care


Screening and Monitoring

Blood pressure, blood sugar, weight checks


Gynecology services 
Allergy Injections
Clinical laboratory services

Urine pregnancy tests, blood glucose, rapid flu, rapid strep, urinalysis, lab draws
