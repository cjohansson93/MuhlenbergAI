A Picture of Health



Muhlenberg student Olivia Toner '20 spent her summer in Lehigh Valley Health Network's Oncology Lab, where she worked alongside health professionals to map treatment strategies and record patient outcomes.


By:



  Jonathan Schlegel  



	 Tuesday, September 10, 2019  03:48 PM


Toner, a biochemistry and Spanish major preparing for medical school, worked with the physicians at LVHN to assess the delivery of care for patients under treatment for bladder cancer. Her contributions helped the hospital network better understand how their approach to patient care impacted patient outcomes.
