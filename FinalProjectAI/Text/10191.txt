Career Center Stories: Jordan Cimring '20



Jordan Cimring '20 shares how the Muhlenberg Career Center provided him with guidance that took him from his first internship application to the dream job he'll start after graduating in May.


By:



  Jonathan Schlegel  



	 Friday, February 7, 2020  09:13 AM


Cimring, an economics and finance double major and member of the Muhlenberg men's soccer team, began working with the Muhlenberg Career Center his first year on campus and continued through the next four years. Now, months before he receives his diploma, he knows where his future begins.
The Career Center staff is committed to working with each Muhlenberg student, from the moment they step on campus to graduation and their careers as alumni. Visit the Career Center website to learn how they connect with Muhlenberg community members to produce powerful outcomes.
