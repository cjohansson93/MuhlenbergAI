Muhlenberg Useful Living Essentials (M.U.L.E.)
The Muhlenberg Useful Living Essentials (M.U.L.E.) Community Cabinet provides non-perishable food items and hygiene products to the student body. All Muhlenberg students are welcome to take items from the cabinet.
The M.U.L.E. Community Cabinet was established in an effort to combat food insecurity among students. Food insecurity is defined as ânot having reliable access to sufficient and nutritious foodâ according to the College and University Food Bank Alliance. The Cabinet offers supplemental non-perishable food items, personal hygiene products, and school supplies to students in need.
If you would like to donate items to our cabinet, please see the listing below of suggested dontation items. All donations must be unopenned to be accepted. Please email mulecabinet@muhlenberg.edu to schedule drop off donations.
If you or your student group would like to host a donation drive please email mulecabinet@muhlenberg.edu
 
Monatary dontations to the M.U.L.E. Community Cabinet can be made using our fundraising page at:
M.U.L,E Community Cabinet Fundraising
 
User guidelines
The M.U.L.E. Community Cabinet is open to all students with a valid Muhlenberg ID number. Students who enter the Community Cabinet will be able to take as many items as they need.
