The Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society (HMMS) was founded in 1967 to recognize a very special group of donors. Each year, alumni, parents and friends who make a leadership gift of $1,500 or more automatically become members.
Member Benefits
HMMS members are invited to the annual An Evening of Distinction gala, are recognized by name in the  Philanthropy Report, receive special premiums throughout the year and have access to concierge service for individual assistance.
HMMS Leadership Levels
The Chairman's Circle $25,000 and above
The President's Circle   $15,000 to $24,999
The Trustee's Circle    $10,000 to $14,999
The Dean's Circle    $5,000 to $9,999
The Professor's Circle    $2,500 to $4,999
The HMMS Circle    $1,500 to $2,499
Young Alumni HMMS
Young alumni (graduates of the last decade) can join with a gift of $100 multiplied by the number of years since graduation. Take a leadership role by joining the HMMS and become a guiding force in the future of the College.
Student HMMS
Students can begin supporting alma mater and each other with a gift of just $48.
Joining the HMMS 

Gifts count for the fund year July 1 - June 30 and can be made in monthly or quarterly installments
Gifts can be in the form of a check, credit card, securities or other assets
Pledges are payable through June 30
Corporate matching gifts are included as part of your total giving
