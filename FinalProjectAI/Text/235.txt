Election Information
PA primary is May 17th 2022!
***If you plan to vote in the PA primary and don't live in PA make a plan to request a mail in or absentee ballot! The primary occurs in the week after finals week, so if you won't be here, make a plan!
PA VOTING INFORMATION
In Pennsylvania you must:
Register 15 days prior to Election Day
Have lived in the state for 30 days
Be a US Citizen
The polling location for all students registered with their box number is Seegers Union.
Not sure if you're registered in PA? 
Check your status here. You can register to vote online, but you must include your box number and if you are not from the state of PA you have to MAIL IN the completed application with your signature. The OCE in Seegers Union will happily mail in your form with enough advance notice prior to the registration deadline.
How to register in PA? 
Check out this helpful video and click through this presentation or DM the BergVotes instagram.
Voting deadlines to remember: 
Monday May 2nd, 2022 (Voter Registration Deadline)
Tuesday May 10th, 2022 (Absentee/Mail-In Ballot Applications Due)
Tuesday May 17th, 2022 (Primary Election- Absentee/Mail-In Ballots due by 8 pm)
 
ABSENTEE/MAIL-IN INFORMATION FOR PA
Students registered to vote at their on-campus address who are from out of state and not on campus should request an absentee ballot using this form and mail it to the Lehigh County Voter Office at: 17 S. 7th St., Allentown PA, 18101-2401 to vote in the May 17th, 2022 election. If you are from PA, you can apply online for an absentee ballot with a valid state ID. Applications must received by Tuesday May 10th, 2022.
If you vote by mail or absentee they MUST receive your ballot by 8 pm on May 17th, 2022!
