All students who are considering prehealth and/or a major in the natural sciences are required to take the chemistry placement exam. This exam includes a short questionnaire which will then lead you to the chemistry placement exam. Once completed, you will continue forward to submit the exam online. Note: That your exam is not submitted until the confirmation at the end.
