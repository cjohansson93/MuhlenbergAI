Prospective Student Need-Based Aid Applicants

Muhlenberg College uses preferential packaging when determining an award
Deadlines are important-meet them or beat them!
Copy EVERYTHING including electronic receipts
Verify the receipt of documentation by checking your Financial Aid Online Account
Own the process to maximize your opportunity for aid
We will do all we can to help

Step 1: Log into your Muhlenberg College Financial Aid Online Account and complete the Muhlenberg College Scholarship and Aid Application after October 25, 2021 and before April 15, 2022
Step 2: Student and Parent should EACH obtain an FSA ID. Click here for instructions on how to create an FSA ID. 
Step 3: After October 1, 2021 and by April 15, 2022 complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) the Muhlenberg College FAFSA Code is 003304.  Be sure you are using your correct social security number when completing this form.
Step 4: After you have completed and submitted the FAFSA Form (Step 3), please check your Muhlenberg College Financial Aid Online portal regularly. Once we review your FAFSA, there may be additional documentation required. You will be able to upload any requested documents directly to the Muhlenberg Financial Aid portal.
