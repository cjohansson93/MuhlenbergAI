Men's Lacrosse Upended by No. 13 St. Mary's
 Sunday, March 9, 2014







					 




The Muhlenberg men’s lacrosse team couldn’t keep up with nationally ranked St. Mary’s (Md.) and was dealt an 18-6 defeat.

Senior Casey Gill and sophomore Steven Casella scored two goals apiece for the Mules (1-4) in a game originally scheduled to be played Saturday in Maryland. It was Gill’s second career multi-goal game and the third of the season for Casella.

Juniors  Jason Mertz (right) and Pat Farrell each had a goal and an assist. Freshman Elliot Dennis split his 20 faceoffs and scooped up a game-high seven ground balls against the Seahawks (3-2), who came in tied for 13th in Division III.
