IT Support Portal



If you wish to meet in person, please contact the OIT Help Desk at 484-664-3566 to schedule an appointment, Monday-Friday 8:00am-5:00pm or email us: helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu.

The Office of Information Technology (OIT) is located on the lower level of Ettinger Hall.  The OIT Help Desk is now in ONE convenient location providing tech help for students, faculty and staff. 




OIT Help DeskEttinger Hall, Room 002 484.664.3566 helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu




Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday, from 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.Evenings and weekends may be staffed by student help desk associates during the academic year (schedules may vary). 
The Main Office is in Suite 014 and is open Monday - Friday, from 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
