Give the gift of the performing arts!



Gift certificates are available online, in person, or on the phone, for any dollar amount. Gift certificates never expire, and the value can be applied to the purchase of tickets for either Muhlenberg Summer Music Theatre shows or Theatre & Dance Mainstage productions.
Purchase Online or call the box office at 484-664-3333.
