Individual Counseling
Counseling Services utilizes a short-term therapy model of counseling. This means that sessions will focus on the issues/concerns at hand. If ongoing, continuous therapy is required, the student will be referred to a professional in the community. If a student requires medication management, student may be referred for a psychiatric consultation.
Our counselors have training and experience with many student concerns, some of which are listed below:



Academic Concerns
   
Relationship Issues


Adjustment Issues
        
Roommate Issues


Alcohol/Drug Use and Abuse 
 
Self-esteem


Anxiety/Panic
 
Self-injury


Depression
 
Sexual Assault


Eating Issues/Body Image
 
Sexuality Identity Issues


Family Concerns
 
Social Injustice


Grief/Loss
 
Stress Management


 
 
Suicidal Thoughts/Behaviors



 
How to Make an Appointment
To make an appointment: You may request a consultation appointment by going to your Student Portal --> Log in to your OneLogin Page --> Click on the PyraMed Health Portal Tile --> My Forms (at the top of the page) --> Select "Consultation Request Form" and fill out your information before submitting the form. Once submitted, a representative from the Counseling Department will contact you within 24 hours via Secure Messaging (also located on your OneLogin) to schedule an appointment. You can also call 484-664-3178 and we will assist you in scheduling a consultation time.
