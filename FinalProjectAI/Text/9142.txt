Teaching Interests
Having dedicated my career to accounting, I like to impart my passion for accounting to my students.  Accounting is the language of business and is, therefore, integral to communicating business information.  I strongly believe that accounting can unlock many doors for students, regardless of career choice, and I intend to provide the key!
Research, Scholarship or Creative/Artistic InterestsMy research interests include exploring taxation and public policy as well as teaching accounting at the college level.
