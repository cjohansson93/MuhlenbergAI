MUHLENBERG COLLEGE FAMILY WEEKEND
 OCTOBER 29-31, 2021
The registration form for Family Weekend is now open! Please use this form to indicate which events you plan to attend and to upload your proof of COVID-19 vaccination. Children under 12 do not need to register.
We thank you for your continued support of Muhlenberg College! If you have any questions regarding Family Weekend programming, please email studentactivities@muhlenberg.edu or orientation@muhlenberg.edu.
If you have vaccination questions, please contact covid-19@muhlenberg.edu. 
We welcome parents to dine in the Wood Dining Commons, complements of Muhlenberg Dining Services, during the following meal periods:
Friday dinner:  4:30 PM - 7:00 PM
Saturday brunch:  10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Saturday dinner:  4:30 PM - 7:00 PM
Sunday brunch:  10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Sunday dinner:  4:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Check out the full schedule below!
