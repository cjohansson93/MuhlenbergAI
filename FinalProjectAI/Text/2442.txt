Marisa Rubin '17
Marisa Rubin ’17 is a media & communication major, with a focus on public relations. Marisa hopes to continue her work in public relations and tie in her love of music after she graduates by working for small record labels.
Marisa is a spring 2016 intern for the office of public relations at Muhlenberg College and is also involved in Relay for Life and as a campus representative captain for Serengetee, a non-profit clothing company.
