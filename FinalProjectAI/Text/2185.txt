Auditions for Departmental Theatre ProductionsFall 2021
Preliminary auditions for all three Departmental theatre productions for Fall 2021 will be held via one initial video submission. Video submissions will be due no later than Tuesday, Aug. 31 at noon ET.
Callbacks will be held in person, on campus, Sept. 1-5. Casting will be complete by Sept.6, and rehearsals will begin the same day.

Monologue Requirements

Perform a 60- to 90-second monologue of your choice, in any style, in any period.
Choose a monologue NOT from one of these plays.
Your monologue does not need to be memorized.

Requirements for video submissions

Between one and two minutes in length
Shot in landscape mode (i.e. with your phone sideways,)
Labeled with your first and last name and the date, e.g. MerylStreep-123120. (If you suspect that other people have the same first and last name, please feel free to include a middle initial.)
Please slate your name at the beginning and the end of your video and include one fun fact about yourself (e.g. after winning the Oscar in 1980, Meryl accidentally left it in the ladiesâ room during the Governorâs Ball).
We prefer that you upload your video via the Google submission form. If you run into difficulty, you can upload to another file-hosting site (Google Drive, YouTube, Vimeo, etc.) and include a URL in the form.

Important Information

Each student may perform in a maximum of one theatre production and one dance production in the fall semester.
You cannot perform/choreograph in both "Reset: New Dances" and "The Saint Plays."
You cannot perform in "The Saint Plays" and be involved with Candlelight Carols.

All preliminary video submissions are due by noon ET on TUESDAY, AUG. 31. NO EXCEPTIONS.
