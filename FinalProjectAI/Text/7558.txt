Wrestlers Defeat Division I Opponents at East Stroudsburg Open
 Monday, November 17, 2014 - page 3







					 




The Muhlenberg wrestling team held its own against a high level of competition at yesterday’s East Stroudsburg Open.

Three of the four Mules who competed won at least one match against a Division I opponent.

Junior Che Chengsupanimit (left) and freshman Ben Peck each went 2-2.
Chengsupanimit defeated two Division I foes at 149, pinning a Hofstra wrestler in his first bout and scoring a 
decision against an opponent from Lehigh in the consolation bracket.

Peck won his first two bouts at 133, decisioning wrestlers from Brown and Gloucester County College.

Freshman Jake Gordon notched a 13-6 decision against a wrestler from Division I Franklin & Marshall in his first bout.
