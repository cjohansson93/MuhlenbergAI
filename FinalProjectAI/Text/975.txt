Driving Tour of Campus
Welcome to Muhlenberg! We are so excited that you are visiting us today, whether you are here on campus or joining us at home. Today, your tour guide is Zaire, a junior here at Muhlenberg who is a double major in theatre and political science.
After you take our driving tour, please fill out this form so we know you visited with us today!
Our tour starts on the front circle. Enter the circle from Chew Street and find a parking space in front of Egner Chapel or Moyer Hall.
Before you start your tour, please note our safety reminders:

As you drive around our campus, please make sure to follow all posted traffic and parking signs, as some of our streets are public streets.
Please be mindful of using your phone or device to access video only when parked.
Our campus is walkable, so please remain alert for pedestrians at all times.
If there is an emergency, please call our Department of Campus Safety and Police emergency line at 484-664-3110. If a response from an ambulance or fire company is needed, dial 911.








Stop 1: Introduction, Egner Chapel, Moyer Hall, Ettinger Hall


To get to stop 2, continue on the front circle and find a parking space near the Haas College Center or Trumbower Hall.

Stop 2: Haas College Center, Trumbower Hall, New Science Building, Front Lawn


To get to stop 3, continue on the front circle and find a parking space near Seegers Union.

Stop 3: Seegers Union, Center for the Arts, Trexler Pavilion


To get to stop 4, exit the circle and turn right onto Chew Street. Turn right onto N. 26th Street, then make the second right into the faculty and staff parking lot past Walz Hall. Make an immediate right to park in the inlet by Walz and Brown Halls.

Stop 4: First-year Residence Halls


To get to stop 5, continue driving through the faculty and staff parking lot, past the soccer field and practice field (which will be on your left). Continue to the larger parking lot, and find a parking space near The Century Arch.

Stop 5: Walson Hall


To get to stop 6, continue driving though the faculty and staff parking lot, past the New Science building, Trumbower Hall, and the Haas College Center, which will be on your right. Turn left and find a parking space near Scotty Wood Stadium and the Life Sports Center.

Stop 6: Life Sports Center Martin Luther Hall, East Hall


To get to stop 7, exit the faculty and staff parking lot the way you came in. Turn right onto N. 26th Street. Turn right onto Liberty Street, and continue driving until you reach N. 23rd Street. On you drive on Liberty Street, the back entrances to Scotty Wood Stadium and the Life Sports Center will be on your right. You will see some of our MILE house options on the left. Once you reach N. 23rd Street, turn right. Then, turn left onto Gordon Street and turn right into the student parking lot. Find a parking spot looking out on N. 23rd Street.

Stop 7: Moyer Lawn


To get to stop 8, exit the student parking lot and turn right onto Gordon Street. Turn right onto N. Leh Street. Turn right onto Chew Street, and turn left onto N. 23rd Street, where youâll enter a small parking lot. Park by the stone wall next to the Multicultural Center.

Stop 8: Multicultural Center, Global Education, Leffell Center for Jewish Life


To get to stop 9, exit the parking lot and turn left onto Chew Street. Make the first left by the large parking lot, and turn left into the parking lot. Find a parking space in this parking lot, where Chew Street is behind you.

Stop 9: Trexler Library


And that concludes the tour! Thank you for joining us today, and safe travels home!
