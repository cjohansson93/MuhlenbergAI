The average federal loan debt for the Class of 2018 is $23,484.11.
Only 3% of Muhlenberg Students are in default.
As of May 10, 2015, you must create a username and password to log on to any government site.  Click here for more directions on how to set up an account.
How do I find my loans? 

Go to NSLDS.ed.gov and log on.   Here you will see a listing of all your current Federal Direct and Stafford Loans.  The loan servicers will be listed for each loan, along with their contact phone numbers and websites.  *Note: NSLDS is not real time.  Check with your loan servicer to see the most recent information.
