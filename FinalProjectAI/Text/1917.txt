Corporate PartnershipsCapstone Projects 
 












The School of Continuing Studies enjoys many strong relationships within our community. Outreach efforts include Capstone Projects completed by our students in the Accelerated Degree Program, and Corporate Partnerships: onsite programs personalized to meet the needs of local organizations.


If you are interested in learning more about any of these opportunities, please contact our office at 484-664-3300 or continuingstudies@muhlenberg.edu.
