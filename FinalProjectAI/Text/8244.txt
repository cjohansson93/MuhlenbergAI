2015 Senior Thesis Exhibition


Showcasing the work of Caroline Bronston, Deborah Carlin, Hannah Cascio, Lucy Mazur-Warren, Colleen Quinn, Erin Sauter, and Micheal Wadas


	 Friday, April 10, 2015  04:20 PM

The Martin Art Gallery proudly hosts the 2015 Senior Thesis Exhibition which will showcase the work of Caroline Bronston, Deborah Carlin, Hannah Cascio, Lucy Mazur-Warren, Colleen Quinn, Erin Sauter, and Micheal Wadas. Their work is the culmination of meeting all the requirements of a two-semester senior seminar course in studio art and focuses on digital photography, acrylic painting and mixed media sculpture.
An opening reception will be held Wednesday, April 29, 4:30 – 6:00 p.m. The exhibition and reception are free and open to the public. Gallery hours are Tuesdays – Saturdays, noon – 8:00 p.m. and by appointment. The exhibition closes Saturday, May 16. For additional information: 484.664.3467.
