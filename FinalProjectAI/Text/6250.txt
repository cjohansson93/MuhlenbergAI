Men's Tennis Falls at TCNJ
 Sunday, March 18, 2012







					 


The Muhlenberg men’s tennis team was shut out at The College of New Jersey, which was ranked 13th in the Northeast Region in the Intercollegiate Tennis
Association preseason rankings.

Two Mules went to a third set in singles play. At No. 1, senior Jordan Rutt came back after losing the first set, 6-0, to win the second, 6-4. The
Lions player captured the match in the third set, 6-2.

Freshman Eric Chavis won his first set at No. 5 but had to retire in the third set after dropping the second, 6-1.
