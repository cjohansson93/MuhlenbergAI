Before Your Advising Day
From all of us at Muhlenberg College, we can't express how excited we are to meet the Class of 2026 this fall. Before your advising session, we ask that you complete the forms below to help your advisor and our staff better understand how we can prepare for your start at Muhlenberg.















First-Year Seminar Selection



Explore options for your First-Year Seminar—small, discussion-oriented courses for first-year students with opportunities to work closely with faculty.




Discover First-Year Seminars



















Course Offerings



Your advisor will work with you to select courses appropriate for your first semester at Muhlenberg.




Explore First-Year Student Courses


















Complete New Student Onboarding in Workday



Workday is Muhlenberg’s student information system, where you will do everything from course registration to updating your personal information. Prior to Summer Advising you must complete the New Student onboarding tasks.




Complete Onboarding



















Online Math and Language Placement Exams



Math and language placement exams ensure that your aptitude and experience are accurately measured before your advising day.




Complete Questionnaire and Test



















Office of Disability Services Form



Request a pre-advising appointment with the Office of Disability Services and learn about submitting your documentation in advance of your appointment.




Schedule a Conversation


















Music Pre-Advising Form



Please complete this form prior to your advising day. The information you provide will be used for placement in ensembles and/or individual applied lessons.




Music Pre-Advising Form


















Online Chemistry Placement Exam



The chemistry exam ensures placement in the correct level for students who are considering prehealth and/or a major in the natural sciences.




Chemistry Questionnaire and Exam
