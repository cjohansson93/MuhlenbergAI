In order for all features of the online exams and questionnaires to function properly, please use a desktop computer.  In addition: 




For the Math Exam, 

You must use any Mozilla 5.0+ browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and, 
you must accept cookies from this site (www.muhlenberg.edu and webapps.muhlenberg.edu), and
scripting must be turned on for this exam site



For the Second Language Exam,

You must meet all requirements specified for the Math Exam as listed above, and
You must have computer speakers for the Listening Comprehension part of the Hebrew language exam





Unsupported: Internet Explorer, Opera (Mac) / Tested/Approved Browsers: Firefox , Safari, Chrome
