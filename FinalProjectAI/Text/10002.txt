 Ilana Levy  Part-Time Lecturer in Hebrew Ettinger 104D Office: 484-664-3348 Home: 484-661-1111 Fax: 484-664-3722

Background B.A., Hebrew University B.J.E., University of Judaism
Ilana Levy is a native of Israel and has been an instructor of Hebrew Language at both Muhlenberg and Lafayette Colleges since 1998. She continues to teach children and adults formally and informally. Mrs. Levy is an active volunteer both in the Allentown community and her synagogue. She also volunteers for KidsPeace teaching Jewish children to read Hebrew in addition to translating in both languages, English and Hebrew.
