Explore Muhlenberg: Spring Open House - April 30



Join us for an exciting day on campus to experience how Muhlenberg opens doors for our students and alumni for a lifetime. Take a tour of campus, meet our students and faculty, learn more about your interests and hear about the admissions and financial aid process.

On-Campus Tours, Info Sessions and Events



We invite you to see our campus in person by scheduling an on-campus tour, information session or both to make the most of your visit. You may also choose to join us for one of our special Summer Saturday events for an even more comprehensive view of our community and academics.

Admissions Interview



Students are welcome to schedule an admissions interview with a member of our admissions staff as part of their application process. These conversations give us a chance to better learn about your talents and plans and answer any questions you may have, too!
