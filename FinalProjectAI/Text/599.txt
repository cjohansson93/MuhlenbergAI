Who are Continuing Studies Students?



For more than 100 years, the Muhlenberg College School of Continuing Studies has understood the needs of adult learners. Muhlenberg College professors and advisors work hand-in-hand with our students to personalize their educational journey. These students include:

Adults who want to start or finish a bachelor's degree
Veterans or active military personnel
Adults with a bachelor's degree who are seeking a second degree or certificate in a specific content area
