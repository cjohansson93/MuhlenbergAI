men’s golf second atopening tournament


The Muhlenberg men’s golf team opened up its 2009-10 season by placing second at the Moravian Fall Invitational.

The Mules finished two strokes off the lead, trailing only Centennial Conference rival Franklin & Marshall, in their first trip to Woodstone Country Club.

Zachary Oyer picked up where he left off last year, when he led Muhlenberg in stroke average. The junior placed second in the 97-golfer field
with a 2-over-par 74.

Freshman Brandon Davis shot a 77 in his collegiate debut to tie for seventh, and senior Biff Slocum (left) came in one stroke behind.
Junior Jordan Carpenter was the Mules’ fourth scorer, carding an 80.

In seven fall tournaments over the last two years, Muhlenberg has finished second five times.
