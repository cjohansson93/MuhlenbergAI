Chemistry
The major program in chemistry provides a curriculum that accommodates and encourages students with various interests. The major program is organized around a required core of courses in the traditional areas of chemistry. Each course in the core curriculum involves a laboratory component that promote
 


hands-on knowledge of scientific experimentation
the capacity to interpret experimental data
an ability to analyze data statistically
the skill to communicate results


The major also includes elective courses which broaden students' exposure to related areas of science. The chemistry department is accredited by the American Chemical Society.

Students perform experiments during an Honors Organic Chemistry Lab
