Muhlenberg hosts the Morehouse College Glee Club


The renowned choral group will perform in Egner Memorial Chapel on March 17 at 7:00 p.m.


	 Wednesday, March 16, 2016  08:40 AM

The Morehouse College Glee Club was founded in 1911 and is the official choral group of Morehouse College, a private, all-male, historically black liberal arts college located in Atlanta, Ga. The group has a storied tradition of national performances, including at the funeral of Martin Luther King, Jr. and for several sitting U.S. presidents.
Tickets to the 7:00 p.m. event in the College’s Egner Chapel are available for purchase for $10 at the Muhlenberg College box office or online. The event is free to members of the Muhlenberg College community with a college ID.
The event is presented by Student Activities, Division of Student Affairs, Chaplain's Office, President's Office, Office of Community Relations, Theatre & Dance Department, Office of Multicultural Life and Music Department.
