volleyball wraps up texas trip









The Muhlenberg volleyball team closed out its trip to Texas with a 3-0 loss to Division II St. Edward’s.

After losing the first two sets, 25-11 and 25-17, the Mules (4-8) had a good chance to pull out the third set, taking a 21-19 lead. But the Hilltoppers (12-5)
ran off the next five points and secured the match with a 25-22 victory.

During the trip, Muhlenberg hung out at the University of Texas, attended the Texas vs. Texas-El Paso football game and toured the Alamo.
