Muhlenberg Athletics - Summer Check-In



What does a Mule do in the summer? Over the next few weeks we'll look at our athletes' busy summers.


	 Thursday, August 3, 2017  07:56 AM

Muhlenberg student-athletes are busy this summer - on campus, in the Lehigh Valley, in their hometowns and even around the world.
Click on the athlete's name for a picture and description. At least one new story will be added every day.
Luke Wiley, football - Ultimate Tic Tac Toe research Olivia Nacheff, women's lacrosse - brand ambassador/hospitality intern Michael Havkins, men's track & field - travel across United States Carly DeNigris, women's soccer - biology research Michael Silverstein, men's track & field - decision-making research Kristina Qualben, softball - QxQ math research Alyssa Scott, field hockey - Lehigh Valley Health Network intern Jake Gordon, wrestling - venture capital analyst in Tel Aviv Erica Schultz, volleyball - team performance research Julia Leep-Lazar/Emily Davidson, women's track & field - tick research Matt Riebesell, baseball - SNY remote operations intern Rachel Plotke, women's basketball - Planned Parenthood intern Levelle Arnold, men's basketball - virtual reality research Emma Rosenthal, field hockey - Camp Charlie volunteer  Know of someone else who should be on this list? Send an e-mail to mikefalk@muhlenberg.edu.
