Managing your Financial Aid Online Portal
Your Financial Aid Portal is activated once you apply to Muhlenberg College and it will allow you to :

Check the status of your financial aid application by monitoring the document page
Allow access to the Muhlenberg College Scholarship and Aid Application that you must complete
And, when accepted view your financial aid award and accept, decline, or reduce any portion of the award 
The best internet browsers for using your Financial Aid Online Portal are 

Internet Explorer 11
Firefox (Most recent version)
Google Chrome (Most recent version


Access your portal here.
Prospective Students and Current Students needing to -Activate your Financial Aid Online Account


Your user ID :
Applicants for Admission:Your Student ID is the e-mail address you indicated for yourself on the Common App Admission Application.  
Current Muhlenberg College students:Your Student ID is your Muhlenberg College e-mail


Click on First Time User


You will then be asked to set up your security questions and answers and it is case sensitive (Remember these because the Office of Financial Aid Staff cannot see the questions and answers you set up)


Create your password - Your Password must be at least 9 characters in length.  It must contain at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number and 1 special character (!@#$%^&*).  Your password will expire every 90 days and you must have 5 unique passwords before you can recycle one. (The Office of Financial Aid staff cannot see your password so make a password you can remember)


Accept the Terms and Conditions of the site.


You will then be returned to the main login page.  Login with your e-mail address and your newly created Password and hit SUBMIT.


In the upper left had side of the page you should see MENU.  You can click here to pick the page you would like to go to.


 
Returning Users


Remember the Student ID for Applicants for Admission is the studentâs e-mail address that was provided on  the Common App Admission Application.  Current Students will use their Muhlenberg College e-mail.


Type in your password and Click on SUBMIT.


If you have forgotten your password, click on Forgot Your Password.  You will be prompted to enter your Student ID e-mail address and answer your security questions.


After 6 unsuccessful attempts to log in your account will be locked.  Please contact the Office of Financial Aid at 484-664-3175 or finaid@muhlenberg.edu and someone will be happy to unlock it for you.  
See code IA for assistance with the most common mistakes.
 
As always, if you have any questions or concerns or need any help with the application process, please let us know! We may be reached at 484-664-3175 or finaid@muhlenberg.edu
