Wrestlers Step Up in Level:
The Muhlenberg wrestling team took a step up in competition at the 43rd East Stroudsburg Open, one of the oldest open wrestling tournaments in the nation.
Close to 400 wrestlers, the great majority not from Divisions III, competed.

The Mules drew the likes of Maryland, Lehigh, Rutgers and Edinboro in their bouts. Of the eight Muhlenberg wrestlers entered, two (sophomores
John Sternlicht and Nick Pallotta picked up a win apiece in their respective consolation brackets.
