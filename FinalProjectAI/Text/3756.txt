Today’s Result


MENS GOLF. The Mules held steady on day two of the Guy W. Kuhn Invitational, finishing with a two-day total of 616 to finish eighth out of 16 teams. Brandon Davis led Muhlenberg with a score of 150, including an even-par 72 in the second round, to finish in a tie for 11th place. Zachary Oyer also fired consecutive rounds in the 70s, to finish in a tie for 26th.

Centennial Conference rival McDaniel (594) beat Carnegie Mellon in a playoff to win the tournament hosted by Allegheny.
