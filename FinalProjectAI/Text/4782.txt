Today’s Result


The Muhlenberg track and field teams sent three runners to the Swarthmore Last Chance Meet. Junior Bobby Torphy ran an outdoor career-best 1:53.45 in the 800, and
senior Patrick Thornton closed out his career in the same event by running a season-best 1:56.84. Senior Jennifer Lynch competed in the 1,500 meters. The
Mules have another last chance meet at The College of New Jersey next Thursday.
