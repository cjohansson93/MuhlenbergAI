Register Now For Summer II Courses At The Wescoe School



The second summer term of the Wescoe School of Muhlenberg Collegeâs liberal arts program will begin Wednesday, July 6 and end on August 16. 


	 Thursday, June 2, 2005  01:59 PM

Classes are offered both during the day and on weeknights. Registration has begun and will continue until the first day of classes. 
Summer study provides opportunities for high school students to get a head start on their college education, for traditional students from other institutions and adult students to move closer to their degrees and for community members to pursue their interests. Courses in the Summer II semester will be available in the following disciplines: anthropology, art history, biology, business administration, economics, English, history, mathematical sciences, philosophy, political science, psychology, religion and sociology.
To receive a catalog or more information about these courses or any of the Wescoe programs: bachelor’s and associate’s degrees, traditional or accelerated study, teacher certification, financial aid opportunities, tuition reimbursement and more, please call 484-664-3300 or visit www.muhlenberg.edu/wescoe.
