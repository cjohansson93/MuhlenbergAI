Ask a Librarian



Library Home|Research|Ask A Librarian





Whether you are starting your first research project or have written a dozen papers already, you can benefit from a one-on-one session with a Muhlenberg College librarian.

Ask relevant, significant questions
Discover new research tools and information
Search the library databases and other resources strategically
Narrow a topic via examination of resources
Evaluate resources for appropriateness
Cite sources in the correct style for your discipline
Present your work using modern technology
