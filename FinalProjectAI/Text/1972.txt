Airport Shuttle Service for the Philadelphia and Newark Airports 2021/2022
Shuttles to Lehigh Valley International Airport also known as ABE is listed under Scheduled Breaks and is free.

Shuttle service for our students who have a need to travel using the Philadelphia and Newark Airports. The times listed below are for either airport. 
The fee for the airport shuttle is $75.00 each way. Reservations, should be made at least one week prior to travel. Your reservation will be confirmed by email. Payments will be made to the Business Office, Cashier Window located in the Haas building. Payments can be made by personal check, cash, or money order made payable to Muhlenberg College. Payments can be mailed to Muhlenberg College, Attention Business Office 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, PA 18104.
Students must arrive 15 minutes prior to departure time.
Philadelphia and Newark Airport Shuttle Schedule
Submit your Reservation Request Online
Cashiers Windows hours are:
Monday and Friday 10:30am - 11:30am Wednesday 10am - 11:30am Monday through Friday 1pm - 1pm
In order to reserve a seat on the shuttle, please submit your reservation request. If you have any further questions, you may contact Shuttle Steve at (484) 664-3449.
