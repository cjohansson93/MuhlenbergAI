Wrestling Ends Two-Year Drought
 Saturday, November 9, 2013 - page 3







					 





The Muhlenberg wrestling team won its first dual match in more than two years, defeating Mount St. Vincent by a 30-18 score.

The Mules’ last dual win came against Yeshiva late in the 2010-11 season. 


Only four bout were contested in the match, with each team winning two, but Muhlenberg was able to fill eight of the 10 weight classes and
picked up four forfeit wins.

Sophomore Che Chengsupanimit and senior Andrew Tullo won their bouts. Chengsupanimit edged his opponent at
157, 6-5, while Tullo earned the first win of his career with a 7-3 decision at 197.

The Mules lost to West Virginia Tech (51-3) and Southern Maine (59-0) in their other matches in the quad meet. Chengsupanimit won at 149 against West Virginia Tech.
